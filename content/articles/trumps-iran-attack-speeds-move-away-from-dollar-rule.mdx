Trump’s attack on Iran, called Operation Epic Fury, shows the US using force and sanctions, pushing other countries to reduce reliance on the dollar. The US dollar has weakened, and central banks are moving to other currencies, especially China’s yuan. Europe and the BRICS are building digital currencies and swap lines to protect themselves. This shift could hurt the US, which benefits from low borrowing costs through the dollar.
