Donald Trump criticized UK Prime Minister Keir Starmer for not rushing the UK into supporting US‑Israeli strikes on Iran. Trump said the UK no longer needed to send aircraft carriers, even though the UK had agreed to let US forces use bases in Fairford and Diego Garcia for limited defensive missions. The UK is also prepping its carrier HMS Prince of Wales for possible deployment to the Middle East, while Starmer’s Labour party remains cautious about military involvement.
