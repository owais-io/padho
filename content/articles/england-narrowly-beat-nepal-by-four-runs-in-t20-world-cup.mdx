England narrowly beats Nepal in a tense T20 World Cup game, winning by four runs. Nepal chased 185, but fell short as Sam Curran sealed the victory. The match saw Nepal’s captain Rohit Paudel and Dipendra Singh Airee build a thrilling partnership, while England’s bowlers were costly. Will Jacks’ strong finish and Curran’s final over gave England the win, keeping their tournament hopes alive.
