The Bondi beach shooting that killed 15 people and wounded 22 has been called a terrorist act. Two men are suspected: Sajid Akram, from Hyderabad, whose family says he was not radicalised, and his son Naveed, who is still in hospital and could be charged. The attack hit a Jewish Hanukah event. Victims included rabbis, a Holocaust survivor and a young girl named Matilda.
