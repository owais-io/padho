India won the fourth T20 match against England at Old Trafford, clinching a 3‑1 series win – their first ever in England. England batted poorly and didn’t hit many boundaries. India’s fielding was strong, and openers Shafali Verma and Smriti Mandhana set a good base. India’s batters Harmanpreet Kaur and Jemimah Rodrigues finished the chase. England lost key player Nat Sciver‑Brunt to injury, and Indian bowler Deepti Sharma reached 300 wickets.
