Pakistan launched airstrikes on Kabul, Kandahar and other provinces, saying the Taliban were “in open war.” The attacks followed Afghan forces firing on Pakistani border posts. Pakistani officials claim many Taliban officials were killed, while Afghan authorities say Pakistani mortar fire hit civilian areas, including a refugee camp. The two neighbours have long clashed over militants and failed peace talks, raising fears that the violence will keep spiralling.
