Talks between US and Iran officials are happening in Muscat, Oman, with Oman acting as mediator. The focus is Iran’s nuclear program, but the US also wants to discuss missiles and its support for regional groups. Iran insists on talking only about nukes. Tensions are high after protests in Iran and a US buildup of forces near the Gulf. These talks aim to ease the danger of a clash.
