Iran’s Supreme Leader Mojtaba Khamenei was wounded in a February attack that also killed his father, former Supreme Leader Ali Khamenei, and five other family members. Cyprus ambassador Alireza Salarian said the strike hit their home in Tehran, leaving Mojtaba with leg, hand and arm injuries and placing him in hospital. The attack coincided with U.S. airstrikes on Iran and has kept the new leader out of public view as officials warn of possible assassination.
