US and Iran are back at the negotiating table over Iran's nuclear program. Tehran says a deal is possible if Washington allows it to enrich uranium at low levels, stop missile restrictions, and keep sanctions relief. President Trump has pushed back on these points, threatening more conflict. The talks will take place in Geneva, with UN inspectors present, and could decide Iran’s future sanctions status.
