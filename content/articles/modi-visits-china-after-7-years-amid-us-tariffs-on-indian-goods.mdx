Prime Minister Narendra Modi is traveling to China for the first time in seven years. He will meet Chinese President Xi Jinping and Russian President Vladimir Putin while the U.S. has doubled tariffs on many Indian goods because of India’s oil purchases from Russia. The tariffs hurt India’s big export market, so Modi is also looking to boost trade with Japan and other countries. The trip shows India’s effort to balance its ties with the U.S., China and Russia.
