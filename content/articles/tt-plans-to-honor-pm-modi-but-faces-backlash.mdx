Trinidad and Tobago will give Indian Prime Minister Narendra Modi its top award while he visits. Hindu community in the country welcomed it, seeing it as a sign of shared heritage. But the largest Muslim group, ASJA, said they oppose the honour because of Modi’s record on human rights. They worry about religious intolerance and past incidents in Gujarat. The visit shows India‑Caribbean ties, but also reveals split opinions among locals.
