India hosted the World Para Athletics Championships in Delhi, winning 22 medals and celebrating a record performance. The event drew few spectators, and some controversies emerged, such as Pakistan’s boycott and the International Paralympic Committee lifting the ban on Russian and Belarusian athletes. Britain finished seventh with 25 medals, including new champions. Several world records were set, but the uneven competition and low crowds highlighted challenges for the sport.
