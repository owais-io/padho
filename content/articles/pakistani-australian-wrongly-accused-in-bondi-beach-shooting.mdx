Naveed Akram, a 30‑year‑old Pakistani living in Australia, was mistakenly shown as a Bondi beach shooter after his picture was shared online. The false posts spread fast, claiming he was one of the attackers. Akram said he felt shocked, scared and was forced to quit social media. He recorded a video to clear his name, but Facebook and X didn’t flag the misinformation. The mix‑up left him and his wife worried for their safety.
