President Masoud Pezeshkian said Iran would stop striking neighboring countries if they agree not to let US bases or their airspace be used against Iran. The move was met with anger inside Iran, with the military seeming to reject the pledge and some hardliners calling for a new supreme leader. Meanwhile attacks on Gulf states and US facilities continue, and the debate shows deep power splits in Tehran.
