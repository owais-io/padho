President Masoud Pezeshkian told Iran’s foreign minister to push for fair talks with the United States, as both sides gear up to send senior diplomats to Istanbul for talks about Iran’s nuclear programme. Meanwhile, the U.S. has sent a large naval fleet to the region and President Trump says negotiations are happening, hoping to avoid military strikes. The talks come after Iranian protests that have killed thousands, and the U.S. warns of a possible regime change.
