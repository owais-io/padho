England beat Sri Lanka by 51 runs in a T20 World Cup group match. The English batting lineup struggled, scoring just 146, but their bowlers, especially Will Jacks, made up the difference with key wickets. Sri Lanka fell apart early, losing 5 wickets for 34 runs in the powerplay and finishing at 95. England now has two games left, with the next match against Pakistan on Tuesday, and are one win away from the semi‑finals.
