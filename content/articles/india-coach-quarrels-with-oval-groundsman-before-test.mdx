India coach Gautam Gambhir got into a heated argument with Surrey’s head groundskeeper Lee Fortis over how the team could use the Oval pitch before the fifth Test. Gambhir accused Fortis of being arrogant and told him he could not dictate the training area, while Fortis warned Gambhir he could file a complaint. Team coach Sitanshu Kotak tried to calm the situation. The row added to a tense series where India trails 2‑1 and England has a break.
