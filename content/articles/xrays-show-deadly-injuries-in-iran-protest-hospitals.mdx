X‑ray and CT images from a major Iranian hospital, taken in one night during the January protest crackdown, show dozens of people wounded by shotgun pellets and live bullets. The scans reveal severe head, chest, and genital injuries—many likely fatal or leaving permanent disability. Experts say the pattern points to deliberate targeting by security forces. The pictures give hard evidence of the violence and the scale of the bloodshed.
