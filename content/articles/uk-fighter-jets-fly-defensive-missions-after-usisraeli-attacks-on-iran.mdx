After the US and Israel bombed Iranian targets, the UK kept its fighter jets in the region. The RAF is on standby from bases in Qatar and Cyprus to shoot down drones and missiles that could hit allies. Britain didn’t join the attacks but warned UK nationals in the Gulf to stay safe and said it would defend allies if needed. Starmer said the UK would not join the fight but would support a negotiated solution.
