England beat India by five wickets in a tense 5th T20 match at Edgbaston, chasing 168 in the final over. Sophie Ecclestone made the winning run on the last ball after an Indian misfield. England’s batting struggled earlier, but key contributions from Danni Wyatt‑Hodge, Tammy Beaumont and a strong bowling spell by Charlie Dean turned the game around. The win keeps England in the hunt for the World Cup.
