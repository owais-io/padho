Safyaan Sharif, a veteran Scottish bowler, was suddenly called to the T20 World Cup after Bangladesh dropped out. He had to juggle a delayed visa and was in his dad’s restaurant before the call. After a shaky start, he’s now prepared for a key match against England in Kolkata. Sharif hopes Scotland can use his experience to upset the hosts and keep their World Cup hopes alive.
