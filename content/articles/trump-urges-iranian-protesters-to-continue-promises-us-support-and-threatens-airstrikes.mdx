Trump said the U.S. will help Iranian protestors and may strike Tehran. He urged Iranians to keep protesting, cancelled all talks with Tehran until the killings stop, and threatened 25% tariffs on any country trading with Iran. The U.S. warned citizens to leave as the death toll climbs to around 2,000. European diplomats fear a sustained bombing campaign would be needed to change Iran’s regime.
