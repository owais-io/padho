Two famous Iranian filmmakers, Jafar Panahi and Mohammad Rasoulof, have blasted the government’s recent internet shutdown. They say cutting off phones and internet is a way to hide the violence against protestors. Artists abroad are joining in, using hashtags like #IranianRevolution2026 to shout support. The move forces people inside to be cut off from the world, while the government keeps its hands out. International groups are urged to help keep communication open.
