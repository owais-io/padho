Trump told reporters that Iran told him protesters are no longer being killed or executed, so he is putting a hold on any US military strikes. He said he hasn’t verified the claims but reviewed all options. Meanwhile Iran closed its airspace and warned it might target US bases if struck, causing oil prices to fall slightly. Diplomats urge a diplomatic solution.
