Trump announced he would bomb Iran, claiming it was needed to stop Iran's nuclear program. The piece argues the attack is based on false claims, has no clear goals, and will likely spark chaos, civilian deaths and regional war. The writer compares it to past US wars in Iraq and Afghanistan, warns that Iran could retaliate, and says the U.S. is ignoring international law and allies' cautions. In short, the author says this move is reckless and unnecessary.
