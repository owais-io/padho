Police in Canada warned Sikh activist Moninder Singh of a credible threat to him and his family just before Prime Minister Mark Carney’s trip to India. Singh, who heads the Sikh Federation of Canada, said a police informant flagged danger linked to India. The warning follows the 2023 murder of activist Hardeep Singh Nijjar and a diplomatic spat over accusations that India was involved. Singh fears the PM’s visit could let India cover up new threats.
