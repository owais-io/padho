Three men in Britain have been charged after a series of assaults on two Pakistani critics living in the UK. The attacks started on Christmas Eve, including a man who was punched up to 30 times at his home. Police raided houses in London, Essex and the Midlands, and counter‑terror units said the attacks were highly targeted. The suspects face assault, arson and weapon charges while investigators still look for the motive.
