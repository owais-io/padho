Experts say that bombings of Iran’s nuclear sites could actually push the country to build a bomb. After Trump left the 2015 deal, Iran increased enrichment and built a 440‑kg stockpile of 60 % enriched uranium. Airstrikes in June damaged facilities but left deep underground sites intact, and inspectors were barred. Analysts fear the remaining technology and a possible regime collapse could let Iran make weapons, raising a global terror risk.
