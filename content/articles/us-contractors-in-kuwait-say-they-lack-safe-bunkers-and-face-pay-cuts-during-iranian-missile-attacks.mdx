Workers for a U.S. defense contractor on bases in Kuwait say they have no good bunkers and their pay was cut after Iranian missiles hit the region. They received little info on safety or evacuation, and their hours were reduced to eight a day. Contractors feel left out of the security plan and worry about leaving the country because of strict labor rules.
