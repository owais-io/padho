Trump’s plan to hit Iran with military strikes comes from a long history of U.S. hostility that started with the 1979 revolution. The embassy hostage crisis, the Iran‑Contras scandal, and past U.S. support for the Shah all fuel deep anger in Iran. Now that the Iranian regime has cracked down on protests, Trump wants to topple it, a move that Iranian exiles cheer but many Iranians see as foreign meddling.
