India’s Football Federation said job applications from former Barcelona coach Xavi and Spain legend Pep Guardiola were fake. They had received 170 applications for the head coach spot, cutting them down to 10, then three unnamed finalists. The finalists are Khalid Jamil, Stephen Constantine and Stefan Tarkovic. The new coach will be ready for the Asian Cup qualifiers. India's team is ranked low and just missed World Cup qualifying.
