Florida council member Chandler Langevin posted hateful tweets calling for the mass deportation of Indian Americans. His remarks angered local residents, Indian American groups, and lawmakers of both parties. After protests at city meetings, Palm Bay’s council voted 3‑2 to formally censure him. Langevin said he wanted to spark a debate on immigration, but critics say his language is bigoted and dangerous.
