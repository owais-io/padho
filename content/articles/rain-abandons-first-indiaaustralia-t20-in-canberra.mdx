The first T20 of a five‑match series between India and Australia was called off in Canberra after 9.4 overs because of rain. India were 97 for 1 and in control, with Suryakumar Yadav scoring 39 not out and Shubman Gill 37 not out. Australia had won the toss and chosen to field, but the game was halted before a result could be decided. The series will resume on Friday.
