Large protests across Iran began over economic hardship and have turned into a nationwide anti‑government movement. Reports say more than 2,000 people have died, many of them protesters, and over 16,700 have been arrested. Hospitals are overwhelmed with gunshot injuries, including eye wounds. The U.S. has warned Iran and even threatened military action, while the Iranian government blames the U.S. for inciting violence. Internet services are blocked, and foreign diplomats are condemning the crackdown.
