At a rally in northern Kentucky, former President Donald Trump told supporters the U.S. has taken a big hit on Iran’s military, saying drones are down 85% and factories are being bombed. He also talked about his job‑creating policies and said the 2024 mid‑term elections are vital. Trump praised the Kentucky primary race, supporting a local challenger over a long‑time Republican critic. The crowd cheered as he praised the economy and warned of the Iran war.
