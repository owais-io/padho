After a new wave of Iranian missiles, Israeli people and politicians all say the country must finish the fight. Everyone—from students to senior leaders—tells the government to keep striking Iran. The war has become normal, with sirens and shelters a daily routine. Even families in Jerusalem rush to safe places. Some tourists are scared, but most Israelis see it as necessary for security. The attacks happened just before Purim celebrations.
