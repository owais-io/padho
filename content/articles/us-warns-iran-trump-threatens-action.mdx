U.S. officials warned that Iran could launch missiles and are building a nuclear program. Senator Marco Rubio gave lawmakers a classified briefing about Iran right before President Trump used his State‑of‑the‑Union speech to say Tehran would never get a nuclear weapon. Meanwhile, the U.S. has deployed a new aircraft carrier and many warships to the Middle East and is ready to strike if talks fail. Senators urged caution.
