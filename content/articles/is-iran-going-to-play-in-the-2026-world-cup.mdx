Trump’s recent tweets said Iran shouldn’t play at the 2026 World Cup, but FIFA says it wants the team in. Iran’s sports minister said the country would not join because of security worries. FIFA can fine Iran or ban it if it withdraws, and it could replace Iran with Iraq or the UAE. The issue remains unresolved until FIFA’s congress on 30 April.
