Iran's Revolutionary Guards arrested top reformist leaders, including allies of President Masoud Pezeshkian, after protests that left thousands dead. The arrests aim to silence critics and prevent criticism of security forces. Officials claim the detainees link to the US and Israel, while the judiciary calls them traitors. Meanwhile, Nobel laureate Narges Mohammadi received a new seven‑year sentence, and Iran’s hard‑line stance remains ahead of nuclear talks.
