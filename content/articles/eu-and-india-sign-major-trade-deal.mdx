The EU and India just signed a huge trade agreement that will open India’s market to European goods and give Indian consumers cheaper cars and wine. The deal, signed after almost 20 years of talks, came as US President Trump’s unpredictable moves made Europe look for new partners. The agreement covers trade, defence, and research, and shows a shift toward a world where countries work together instead of relying on the US.
