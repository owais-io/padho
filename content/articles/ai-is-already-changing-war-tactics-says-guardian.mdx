The Guardian says AI is already changing how wars are fought, with tools used in the Iran crisis. The UN’s chief warns that technology is advancing too fast, and companies like Anthropic and OpenAI face backlash for providing AI that can help pick targets. AI is said to have aided attacks that killed thousands of civilians, raising accountability concerns. Experts call for stronger rules and a treaty to control lethal autonomous weapons.
