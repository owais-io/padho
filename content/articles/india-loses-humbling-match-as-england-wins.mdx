England beat India in a tense match that showed India’s lack of fighting spirit. Early on England’s bowlers, especially Bumrah, ran through India’s batting, taking runs quickly. India’s fielding was shaky, with dropped catches and wrong field placements. England’s batsmen stayed steady, adding runs and pushing the target higher. By the end, India’s morale collapsed, leading to a disappointing, embarrassing defeat.
