Pakistan’s women’s football team, which had no games from 2014‑2022, made a strong comeback in the 2026 Asian Cup qualifiers. They beat Indonesia and Kyrgyzstan, finishing second in their group. The team includes British‑born players like Layla Banaras and Mariam Mahmood, who helped bring new talent and energy. Their success shows the need for more matches and a domestic league.
