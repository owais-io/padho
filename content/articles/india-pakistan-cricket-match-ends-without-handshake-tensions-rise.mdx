In an Asia Cup game, India beat Pakistan but left the field without shaking hands, a move that upset Pakistan. The incident follows a tense clash after a terrorist attack blamed on Pakistan, and the two sides had been close for years when cricket helped keep politics separate. The ICC said no punishment would happen, but the gesture shows sports are now part of the rivalry. They will meet again soon.
