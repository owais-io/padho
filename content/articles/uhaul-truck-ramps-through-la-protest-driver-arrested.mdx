On Sunday, a U‑Haul truck drove into a crowd of people marching in Los Angeles to support the Iranian people. The driver crashed into the demonstrators, smashing the truck’s window and causing police to stop the vehicle a few blocks away. Police arrested the driver, who may face assault charges, and only one protester was lightly hurt. The protest was a stand against Iran’s ruling regime and the U.S.‑backed 1953 coup.
