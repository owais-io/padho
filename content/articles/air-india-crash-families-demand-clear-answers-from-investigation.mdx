Families of the Air India crash are unhappy with the first report, which says the plane’s fuel switches were turned off after takeoff. They want a clearer explanation and accountability to prevent future accidents. The crash, which killed 241 passengers and 19 people on the ground, happened after the plane left Ahmedabad and hit a medical college. The investigation notes no bird strikes, crew were fit, fuel samples were fine, and regulators are assisting.
