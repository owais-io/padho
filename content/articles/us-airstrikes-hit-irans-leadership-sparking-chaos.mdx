US airstrikes have hit Iran, killing about 800 people and bombing key government buildings. The old leader, Khamenei, was killed last week, and Iran is scrambling to choose a new head. Soldiers and officials are fighting over how to respond to the West, while Iran says the U.S. is responsible for the violence. The war is turning into an existential fight, with oil prices rising and Gulf countries uneasy.
