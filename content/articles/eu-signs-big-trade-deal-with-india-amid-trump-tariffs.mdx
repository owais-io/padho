The European Union just signed a major free‑trade agreement with India, cutting tariffs on cars, machinery and other goods. Leaders say it opens India’s huge market to EU firms and gives jobs back to Europe. The deal comes as Trump’s US tariffs hurt EU‑US trade, so Europe is looking for new partners. EU officials and business groups welcomed the pact, saying it will boost growth.
