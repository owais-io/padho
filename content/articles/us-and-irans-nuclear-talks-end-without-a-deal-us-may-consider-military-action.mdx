US and Iran's nuclear talks ended without a deal on Thursday, leaving no progress on key issues like Iran's right to enrich uranium. The Iranian foreign minister said there was “good progress”, but the US team was disappointed with Iran's proposals. Mediators from Oman expect talks to resume in Vienna next week. Meanwhile, Washington is weighing a possible military strike, the biggest in decades.
