On 8 January 2024, Iran shut down the internet as protests spread across the country. In Rasht, thousands marched through the bazaar demanding freedom. Government forces fired on the crowd, set the market on fire, and then shot those who fled the flames. Dozens died and many more were badly burned and wounded. Doctors say the injuries look like a battlefield, not a peaceful protest. Families still struggle to find their loved ones.
