A missile attack on a girls’ school in Minab, southern Iran, has killed 165 people and wounded 96, the latest figure from Iranian state media. The strike is the biggest loss of life in the US‑led campaign against Iran so far. U.S. officials said they are looking into reports of civilian casualties. Iran’s health ministry called it the darkest moment of the conflict, while Malala Yousafzai urged justice for the victims. Outside reporters cannot access the site.
