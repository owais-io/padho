England lost a second straight T20 match to India in Bristol, falling 25 runs short of the 182 target. Nat Sciver‑Brunt, the England skipper, missed most of India’s innings with a hip injury and will be checked for fitness. England’s batting saw Tammy Beaumont hit 50 runs but she was run out, and Sophie Ecclestone also left early. India were powered by Jemimah Rodrigues and Amanjot Kaur, each scoring 50+ to win.
