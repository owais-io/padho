Iran has reopened its airspace after a four‑hour closure that forced airlines to reroute. The move comes as protests continue and the government faces criticism over arrests and a death‑sentence for protester Erfan Soltani, who the judiciary said has not been sentenced to death. Turkey’s foreign minister said it opposes a military strike on Iran, while the U.S. has warned of possible action but is also seeking dialogue.
