Trump‑aligned senators defended the recent US‑Israel strikes that killed Iran’s top leaders, saying the attack was necessary to stop Iran’s missile and nuclear threats. Democrats, led by Senator Mark Warner, called it a “war of choice,” arguing there was no immediate danger to the U.S. and the president should have asked Congress for permission. They warn the strikes could drag the U.S. into a larger Middle East conflict and question what happens after the regime’s top figure dies.
