Pakistani soldiers clashed with the Pakistani Taliban along the Afghanistan border. In two raids, 12 soldiers and 35 militants were killed. The first raid in Bajaur took 22 militants, the second in South Waziristan took 13. The attacks show Pakistan's struggle against a rising militant threat that has been boosted by the Taliban's Afghan takeover. Pakistan’s army urges Kabul to stop its territory being used by fighters and hints at Indian support.
