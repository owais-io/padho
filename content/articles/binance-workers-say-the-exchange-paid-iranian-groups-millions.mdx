Inside Binance employees discovered the crypto exchange moved about $1.7 billion to Iranian‑backed groups, including Yemen’s Houthi rebels, in 2024‑25. They reported the transactions to senior staff, but four of them were fired or suspended. Binance says it followed sanctions and that the investigations were not punished. The founder, Changpeng Zhao, was pardoned by Trump after pleading guilty to money‑laundering charges. The case raises concerns about Binance’s compliance with sanctions.
