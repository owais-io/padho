After weeks of protests, Iran cut off internet and phones, leaving the country in darkness. In that silence, authorities opened fire on crowds, killing thousands and wounding many more. Witness accounts, hospital records and photos show mass graves and patients shot while still in hospital beds. Doctors risked their lives to treat the wounded and report the truth, but many have been arrested or threatened. Iranians demand accountability and the world watches closely.
