Tensions between the U.S. and Iran are rising, and many people in the Middle East are nervous about a possible war. Embassies are pulling staff out, airlines are cutting flights, and governments are telling citizens to stay away. Israel says that if Hezbollah – backed by Iran – attacks, it could hurt Lebanon and spark wider fighting. The whole region is bracing for the fallout of another conflict.
