President Trump has ordered the USS Gerald R. Ford, the world’s biggest aircraft carrier, to sail from the Caribbean to the Middle East. The ship will join the already‑present USS Abraham Lincoln, giving the U.S. more power near Iran. Trump says this will help push Iran into a nuclear deal, but talks in Oman are still uncertain. The move signals a shift from diplomacy to a stronger military show‑of‑force.
