Australia says travelers should be ready for big flight problems because the Iran war has shut airspace in the Middle East. Airports in Qatar, Dubai and Abu Dhabi are closed, and airlines like Emirates, Etihad and Qatar Airways have stopped flights. About 115,000 Australians are in the region, but the government can only offer advice and help from consulates. Those stuck in Dubai or Sydney are waiting for flights to restart.
