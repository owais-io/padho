Many veterans are upset about President Trump's decision to launch airstrikes on Iran. Some say the attacks are a mistake that could hurt U.S. troops and civilians. Others support the move, saying Iran is a danger to America. The news reminds veterans of past wars, like Iraq, and makes them worry about new injuries. Congress members, some veterans, have spoken out, calling the war illegal and dangerous.
