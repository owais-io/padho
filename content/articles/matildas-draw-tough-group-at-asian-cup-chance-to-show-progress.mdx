Australia’s national women's team, the Matildas, have a tough group in next year’s Asian Cup. Their first opponent is South Korea, a side that beat them in the 2018 quarter‑finals. The team has lost key players and is led by new coach Joe Montemurro, who has only three windows to get results. Other group teams include the Philippines, Iran and Jordan. Fans hope the Matildas can show they’ve improved and prove they’re ready for the World Cup.
