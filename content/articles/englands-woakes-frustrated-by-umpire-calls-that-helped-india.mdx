England won the toss and sent India out to bat. Chris Woakes had a good start, taking Rahul’s wicket, but two close umpire decisions saved India’s batsmen, letting them score comfortably. Woakes said it was frustrating because the calls could have changed the game. He talked about the pitch and the challenges for bowlers, and how he’s proud of playing at his home ground.
