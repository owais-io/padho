European stock markets climbed after a New York Times report said Iran’s intelligence agency tried to contact the CIA about ending the Middle‑East war. The news lifted the FTSE 100 and Stoxx 600 and pushed Wall Street futures higher, while the dollar fell and oil prices dipped. Trump announced U.S. tankers would be escorted through the Strait of Hormuz, and U.S. forces destroyed several Iranian ships. The story made investors hopeful, but many remain cautious.
