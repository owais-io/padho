Bill Gates canceled his keynote at the AI Impact Summit in India after fresh revelations about his past friendship with Jeffrey Epstein emerged. The Gates Foundation said he would not speak to keep the event focused on AI. Gates has expressed regret over knowing Epstein. The summit still features leaders like Sam Altman, Sundar Pichai and India’s prime minister, while Gates’ decision highlights growing scrutiny of his connections to the disgraced financier.
