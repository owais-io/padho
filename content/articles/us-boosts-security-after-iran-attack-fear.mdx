US officials have stepped up security after Iran launched an attack, fearing retaliation. Police across the country increased patrols around sensitive sites such as churches, schools, and cultural centers. Experts warn Iran could use drones, cyber‑attacks, or inspire lone‑wolf terrorists. Law‑enforcement agencies have been stretched by other duties, raising readiness concerns. While no panic is advised, officials urge citizens to stay alert and report suspicious activity.
