Khamenei first spoke publicly in 2024, warning that Israel would fall. Later he was targeted by Israeli and US attacks that damaged his compound and likely killed him. The article traces his rise from a student activist to Iran’s Supreme Leader after Khomeini’s death, his building of the IRGC, support for Hezbollah, Hamas, and other groups, and the internal protests that weakened his rule. It ends with his death, ending a long reign.
