UK defence secretary John Healey said that after the US and Israeli airstrikes that killed Iran’s Supreme Leader Ali Khamenei, not many people will mourn him. He called Iran’s regime “evil” and accused it of sponsoring terrorist attacks on the UK. Healey said the UK is not involved in the strikes but will defend its interests in the Middle East. UK MPs are split, with some supporting the US‑Israel action and others condemning it.
