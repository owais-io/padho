A joint US‑Israel strike hit an elementary school in southern Iran, killing more than 100 children. Video of the rubble, checked by fact‑checkers, shows the devastation. Parents raced back to find the scene, while many Iranians feel a mix of fear and hope as the war escalates. The attack comes after months of protests and government crackdowns, adding to growing anxiety about civilian casualties.
