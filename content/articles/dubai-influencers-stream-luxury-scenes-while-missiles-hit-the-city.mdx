Iranian missiles hit Dubai over the weekend, but many popular social‑media creators kept filming and partying. Influencers from the hookah lounge, beach clubs and yachts streamed the strikes on TikTok, Instagram and YouTube, insisting Dubai is still safe and “no one should worry”. Their calm videos sparked outrage, with critics calling them insensitive and ignoring the real danger. The attacks revealed how Dubai’s image as a safe playground has been shaken.
