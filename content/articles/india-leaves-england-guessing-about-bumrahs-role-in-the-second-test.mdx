India is holding off on deciding whether to play star bowler Jasprit Bumrah until the night before the second Test at Edgbaston. The decision depends on the pitch, which might favor batsmen if it stays dry. India’s captain, Shubman Gill, said they’ll only pick the squad after seeing the wicket. The plan is to keep Bumrah fresh for the next Test at Lord’s.
