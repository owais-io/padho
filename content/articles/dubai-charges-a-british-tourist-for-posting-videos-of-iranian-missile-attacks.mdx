In Dubai, 20 people, including a 60‑year‑old British tourist, have been charged under UAE cybercrime laws for filming and sharing a video of an Iranian missile strike. The law says it’s illegal to post content that could upset public security. The tourist, who deleted the video after police asked, faces up to two years in prison, a fine, and possible deportation.
