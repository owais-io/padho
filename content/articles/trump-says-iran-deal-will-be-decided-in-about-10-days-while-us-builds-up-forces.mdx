Trump says an Iran nuclear deal could be made in about ten days, while the U.S. is piling up troops and ships near Iran. He told a press briefing that if talks fail, the U.S. could bomb Iranian nuclear sites. The Pentagon moved two aircraft carriers, fighters, and support planes to the region. Some experts warn this shows a threat to Iran and a possible new war.
