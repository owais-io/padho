Iran says its latest indirect talks with the U.S., held in Geneva with Oman’s help, were more constructive than before. The two sides reached “guiding principles” that could lead to a next meeting about Iran’s nuclear programme. The U.S. hasn’t commented yet and is building up forces in the region. Meanwhile, Iran faces a crackdown on protestors, refuses an independent UN inquiry, and keeps its nuclear sites guarded.
