Farhan Sahibzada, Pakistan’s top scorer at the T20 World Cup, is eager to play in the English Hundred league. Though the four Indian‑owned clubs say they won’t sign Pakistani players, he remains in the longlist of 63 Pakistanis for the auction. The England and Wales Cricket Board warned teams against discrimination, but selection decisions lie with the clubs.
