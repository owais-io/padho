Pakistan decided to keep their T20 World Cup match against India in Colombo, after initially planning to skip it. The move came after Bangladesh pulled out of the tournament, and the ICC and cricket boards had a meeting in Lahore. Bangladesh was spared punishment and got future hosting rights. The decision helps keep the World Cup running and protects the huge broadcast deal.
