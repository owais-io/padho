Shops in Dandenong, a Melbourne suburb, fear a redevelopment that will turn the area into apartments and a new “Little India” precinct. Owner Sudesh Singh worries rents could rise so high they must close before construction starts in 2026. The government promises the precinct will respect the culture, but many shop windows are empty and some owners plan to leave before the project begins.
