Oman is acting as a mediator for talks between the United States and Iran about Iran’s nuclear programme, hoping to prevent a new American attack. The meetings, held separately with Iran’s foreign minister and a US envoy, focus only on nuclear issues, while the US wants to add talks about missiles, support for regional groups and how civilians are treated. The talks come amid US naval build‑up and threats of force, and after Iran’s protests have hit the economy hard.
