Donald Trump told a Washington audience that he will know within about ten days whether he can strike Iran’s underground nuclear site or secure a new deal. He warned that Iran’s nuclear program threatens regional stability and that “bad things” will happen if it continues. Meanwhile the U.S. is adding a carrier strike group to the Middle East and plans a 5,000‑person base in Gaza as part of a new peace board, following stalled talks with Iran in Geneva.
