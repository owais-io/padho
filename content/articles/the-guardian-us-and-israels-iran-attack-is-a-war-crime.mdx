The Guardian says that the United States and Israel’s attacks on Iran are illegal and amount to war crimes. While Europe loudly condemned Russia’s invasion of Ukraine, it has been much quieter about the US‑Israeli strikes that killed civilians, including children at a girls’ school that killed many people. The piece points out that the same countries that fight for international law now show double standards, and warns that ignoring these violations weakens the rules‑based world order.
