Iran fired missiles at Israel and U.S. bases after U.S. and Israeli airstrikes. It said the attacks were retaliation and violated the UN charter. Over 200 people were killed and 700 wounded in Iran. The missiles hit U.S. and allied bases in Qatar, Kuwait, Bahrain, UAE, Saudi Arabia and Jordan, but caused limited damage. Iran warned it would not be gentle and called on other countries to condemn the attacks. The attacks also hit Iranian nuclear talks, heightening tensions.
