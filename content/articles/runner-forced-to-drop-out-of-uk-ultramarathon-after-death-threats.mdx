A woman named Sarah Porter had to leave Britain’s toughest ultramarathon after she received death threats for her fundraiser helping Afghan women. The 108‑mile Montane Winter Spine race organisers pulled her out when the threats grew dangerous. Porter was disappointed but said she’d keep running and her GoFundMe page stays open. Organisers say they worked with police and security teams to keep everyone safe.
