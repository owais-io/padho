Trump slapped India with 50% tariffs on Russian oil, hoping to pull the country closer to the U.S. Instead, India’s leader Narendra Modi flew to China for the Shanghai Cooperation Organisation (SCO) summit, signaling a shift toward Beijing. The event highlighted China and Russia’s growing influence and showed that Washington’s power is slipping, with ripple effects on issues like Ukraine and global alliances.
