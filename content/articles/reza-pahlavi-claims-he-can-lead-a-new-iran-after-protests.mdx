Reza Pahlavi, son of Iran’s former king, says the current regime will fall and he can head a new government. He urges Western powers to help the protesters and warns that the government will collapse soon even without outside help. Pahlavi’s name has appeared at some rallies, but he’s never been back to Iran and many doubt his support. He’s prepared a plan that could keep the monarchy or create a new system.
