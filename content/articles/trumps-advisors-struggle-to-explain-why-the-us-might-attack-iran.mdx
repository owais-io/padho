Trump’s advisors are trying to justify a possible U.S. attack on Iran. He says Iran is building missiles that could reach America, but intelligence reports say it would take a decade for such weapons. The White House has been vague, with officials like Rubio offering half‑answers while the press secretary backpedals on past claims. Iran’s large missile stockpile still worries Washington and Israel, but the threat to the U.S. is less clear.
