England sealed a clean‑sweep of the three‑match T20 series against Sri Lanka on a turning Pallekele pitch. Part‑time bowler Jacob Bethell stole the show, taking 3 for 11 in his final over to finish off the Sri Lankan tail. England’s spinners kept the wickets, while England’s batsmen set a modest target of 128. The win gives England a strong start ahead of the World Cup and puts Sri Lanka down 0‑3.
