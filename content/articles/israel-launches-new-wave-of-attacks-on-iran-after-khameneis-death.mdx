Israel has launched a new series of air attacks on Iranian government sites, including the presidential office in Tehran, after the death of Supreme Leader Ayatollah Ali Khamenei. Iran has fired missiles at U.S. and Israeli targets and is looking for a successor. The strikes have disrupted oil exports and rattled global markets, pushing up prices and prompting the U.S. to consider escorting tankers. Both sides say they will keep striking.
