Bangladesh has decided not to travel to India for the T20 World Cup, so the ICC replaced them with Scotland. The decision comes after a tense relationship between the two countries, including security concerns and political protests. The ICC said it had no evidence of a real danger, but the Bangladeshi board insisted it was unsafe. The move highlights how politics can affect international sports.
