US and Israel launched air attacks on Iran, claiming they killed the country’s top leader. The strikes caused over 200 deaths in Iran and triggered missile attacks on Israel and several Gulf states. Iran hit places like Israel, the UAE, Bahrain, Kuwait, and Lebanon, causing injuries and damage. The world urged calm, and the UN held an emergency meeting. U.S. Republicans praised the strikes, while Democrats condemned them.
