The bandhgala, a traditional Indian jacket, has been a staple of railway staff uniforms since the 1800s. But this month, Railways Minister Ashwini Vaishnaw said it represents a colonial mindset and ordered it removed. A designer from Rajasthan says the jacket is a proud Indian garment rooted in Mughal and Rajput history. The decision has sparked debate about heritage, nationalism and what will replace the jacket.
