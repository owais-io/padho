Since Trump returned, Iran faces both domestic protests and an American naval buildup in the Middle East. The U.S. is pressing for a deal that limits Iran’s nukes and missiles, or it may strike the regime. Experts list three outcomes: a forced compromise, a controlled war, or an uncontrolled collapse that could leave Iran unstable. None of the options promise safety for ordinary Iranians or peace for the region.
