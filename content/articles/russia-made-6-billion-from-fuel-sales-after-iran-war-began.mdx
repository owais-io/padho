Russia has made about €6 billion from selling oil, gas and coal in just the first two weeks after the US‑Israel strike that triggered the Iran war. Most of that extra money – about €672 million – comes from higher oil prices, up 14 % from February. Trump said he might loosen sanctions, which could let Russia sell oil at a better price. The money fuels Russia’s war budget, especially in Ukraine.
