Paramjeet Singh Pamma, a 52‑year‑old Sikh activist in the UK, says police have warned him to install cameras and lock doors after receiving threats linked to the Indian government. He says the UK hasn’t acted fast enough to protect him, citing similar attacks on other Sikh leaders. India labels the Khalistan movement a terrorist threat, but many Sikh activists in Britain fear for their safety. UK officials say they’re aware but remain diplomatic.
