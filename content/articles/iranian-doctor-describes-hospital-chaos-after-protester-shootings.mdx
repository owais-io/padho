A doctor in Tehran says that after the government began shooting protestors with live guns, the city’s hospitals were overwhelmed. He and his staff had to perform dozens of emergency surgeries in a single night, treating people with chest and abdominal wounds that could be fatal. The doctor estimates that more than 20,000 people were killed across Iran during the protests, though official numbers are missing. Fear and blackouts made many people avoid hospitals.
