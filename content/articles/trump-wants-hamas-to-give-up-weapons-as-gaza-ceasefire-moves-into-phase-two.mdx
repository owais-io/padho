Trump posted on Truth Social that Hamas must instantly stop fighting, return the last Israeli captive’s remains, and disarm completely. He says the US will help strip Hamas of its weapons and tunnels, with Egypt, Turkey and Qatar backing the plan. Meanwhile, a new “board of peace” will oversee Gaza’s rebuilding, which could cost over $70 billion. Violence, airstrikes and flooding still threaten civilians.
