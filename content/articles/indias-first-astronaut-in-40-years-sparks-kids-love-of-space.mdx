Shubhanshu Shukla, India’s first astronaut in 40 years, is on the International Space Station and has captured the imagination of schoolkids across the country. In West Bengal, students at Kalash High School took part in a hands‑on space‑science workshop run by the NGO Life‑To and Beyond. With simple paper models and a planetarium app, the children learned about the solar system, the ISS and the idea that space careers are within reach even for rural students.
