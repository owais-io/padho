Trump thinks he can win the war in Iran and that it won’t hurt the U.S. economy. But fighting the Iranians is driving up global oil prices, raising gas costs for everyone. Higher fuel costs feed higher food prices and keep inflation high, which keeps the Fed from cutting interest rates. As Americans see their pockets shrink, their support for Trump falls, putting his reelection at risk.
