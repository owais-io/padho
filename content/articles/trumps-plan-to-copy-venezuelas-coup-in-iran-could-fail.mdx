Donald Trump has said he wants to repeat the 2021 Venezuela takeover, hoping to install a friendly leader in Iran after the death of Supreme Leader Khamenei. Experts warn Iran is very different from Venezuela – it has no diplomatic ties to the U.S., deep anti‑America sentiment, and power lies with the Revolutionary Guards. They say a puppet government would be hard to create and could spark unrest among Iranians who remember past foreign meddling.
