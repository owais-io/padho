England won their first Test against India at Headingley after Ben Duckett scored 149, giving the team a solid start. They chased a target of 371 in the final hour, finishing with five wickets left. England’s bowlers, especially Josh Tongue, took key wickets, while India’s star bowler Jasprit Bumrah was wicketless. Ben Stokes guided England to a 1‑0 series win, highlighting their aggressive batting and disciplined bowling.
