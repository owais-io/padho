Trump is weighing strong military action after protests in Iran kill hundreds. He says the U.S. could hit targets with air strikes, cyber weapons, or more sanctions, and boost online anti‑government support. Iran warns the U.S. not to attack, citing a deadly crackdown that has killed over 500 people and jailed 10,600. Air strikes risk civilian casualties in dense Tehran and could play into Iran’s narrative that the U.S. and Israel are provoking unrest.
