David Lammy, the Labour MP, said a leak from a top‑secret UK national security meeting about the US‑Israel attacks on Iran was a “travesty.” He wants an investigation. The meeting had divided the cabinet over letting the US use British bases for strikes on Iran’s missile sites after Tehran launched retaliatory attacks. Lammy says the cabinet is united but the leak endangers British lives. He also said a UK strike on Iranian targets would be legal.
