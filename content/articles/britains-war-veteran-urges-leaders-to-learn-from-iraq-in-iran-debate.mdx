Calvin Bailey, a former UK war veteran, reminded MPs that the 2003 Iraq war taught important lessons. He said Prime Minister Keir Starmer should use those lessons when deciding if the UK will support US and Israeli strikes on Iran. Starmer has said the government will act lawfully and carefully, drawing on the Chilcot inquiry report. MPs agreed the country must learn from past mistakes instead of re‑debating Blair’s decision.
