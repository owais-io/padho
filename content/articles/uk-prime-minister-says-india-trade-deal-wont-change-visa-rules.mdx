The UK Prime Minister Keir Starmer said that the new free‑trade agreement with India won’t change visa rules, even for students. He flew to Mumbai with more than 120 business and university leaders to strengthen trade. Starmer highlighted that the deal cuts tariffs and could lift UK exports by about 60%. He also said he won’t loosen visa restrictions. The trip also touched on Modi’s close ties to Russia and the UK’s effort to curb Russian oil shipping.
