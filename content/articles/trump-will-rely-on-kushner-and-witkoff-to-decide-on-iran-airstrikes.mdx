Trump is weighing whether to order airstrikes on Iran, and he will rely on advice from his special envoys, Jared Kushner and Steve Witkoff. The two are negotiating with Tehran in Geneva, hoping the Iranian proposal comes this week. Trump says he might hit Iran with limited strikes if talks fail, or a larger strike to force regime change. Iran has warned it will retaliate and even sink a U.S. warship.
