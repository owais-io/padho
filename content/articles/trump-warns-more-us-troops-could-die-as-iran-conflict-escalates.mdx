Trump posted a video on Truth Social telling Americans that three US troops had died after the US‑Israeli strike on Iran and that more could die before the fighting ends. He said the war is a “righteous mission” and called for prayers for the wounded. The Pentagon confirmed three deaths and five serious injuries in Operation Epic Fury. Trump expects the conflict to last about a month.
