England dominated the second day of the Test, smashing India’s lower order with Ben Stokes taking five wickets and Jofra Archer claiming three. The hosts posted 358 runs, while India struggled and were all out for 133, with Rishabh Pant injured. England’s opening duo, Ben Duckett and Zak Crawley, then added 166 runs in just over two hours, putting England back in the hunt and setting up a strong advantage.
