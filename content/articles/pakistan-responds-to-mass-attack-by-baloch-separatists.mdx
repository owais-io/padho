Pakistan’s security forces fought a large assault by Baloch separatists that killed 31 civilians and 17 security staff. Militants attacked military bases, police stations, banks and even the city of Quetta and the port of Gwadar. The chief minister said 145 fighters were killed in 40 hours, while the Baloch Liberation Army claimed responsibility. Pakistani officials blamed India and Afghanistan for helping the militants. The army is clearing the province after the attacks.
