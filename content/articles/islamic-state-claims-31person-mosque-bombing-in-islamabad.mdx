A blast hit a Shia mosque in Islamabad during Friday prayers, killing 31 people and injuring more than 160. The explosion is thought to be a suicide attack, with the Islamic State claiming responsibility. The bombing added to a wave of violence targeting Shia worshippers in Pakistan. Pakistani leaders condemned the attack and promised a full investigation, while hospitals are stretched and need blood donations for the wounded.
