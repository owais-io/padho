UK manufacturers report a strong February, but the biggest worry is oil. Analysts say the price could rise above $100 a barrel because of an Iran‑related conflict that could shut the Strait of Hormuz. The threat has pushed oil to a 14‑month high, made gold jump, and sent European stocks – especially travel and banking – into the red, with the FTSE 100 down 1%.
