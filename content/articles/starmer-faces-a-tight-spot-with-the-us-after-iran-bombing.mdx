Starmer’s UK government is caught between keeping a good relationship with the US and facing pressure from his party to stand up to the president. After Trump carried out air strikes on Iran, the UK has refused to say it helped but won’t openly condemn the attacks. The prime minister says he can’t influence Trump, while his defence secretary has been vague about the legality. Some in Labour want Starmer to take a stronger stand and move closer to Europe.
