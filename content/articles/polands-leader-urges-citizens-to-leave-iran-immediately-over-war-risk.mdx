Polish Prime Minister Donald Tusk told people in Poland and abroad that they should get out of Iran right now because a war could break out in a matter of hours. He said no evacuation help will be available if fighting starts and that the danger is “very, very real.” Tusk urged everyone to cancel any travel plans or leave the country as soon as possible. The warning comes amid rising tensions between Iran and Israel.
