Iran's football federation says the national team might not be able to play in the US World Cup after recent attacks by the US and Israel. The president Mehdi Taj expressed uncertainty, saying hope for the tournament has vanished. Iran is in Group G, set to play in Los Angeles and Seattle. Meanwhile, other football and cricket events in the Middle East have been postponed or cancelled due to safety concerns.
