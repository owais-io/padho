A recent survey of 26,000 people in 21 countries found that most think Donald Trump’s “Make America Great Again” policy has actually helped China grow stronger. Europeans now see the U.S. as a weaker ally, while many countries say China will lead in electric cars and green tech. The poll shows that people around the world are shifting their support from the U.S. to China, and are less hopeful about the U.S. future.
