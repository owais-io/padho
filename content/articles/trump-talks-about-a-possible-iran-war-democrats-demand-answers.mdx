Democrats are pushing back on President Trump’s vague plans to use the military against Iran. After a briefing, senior lawmakers warned that any war would need Congress’s approval and said they’d hold Trump in the next congressional session to explain his strategy. The move comes as Trump has been raising concerns about Iran’s nuclear program and missile tests, while Democrats fear the country could lead to a costly war.
