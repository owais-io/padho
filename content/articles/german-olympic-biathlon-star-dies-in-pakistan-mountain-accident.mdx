German Olympic biathlon champion Laura Dahlmeier, 31, died on July 28 after a rockfall on Laila Peak in Pakistan’s Karakoram range. She was climbing with a partner who escaped unharmed. Rescue attempts were halted because of bad weather and because Dahlmeier had requested that no one risk their lives to recover her body. The mountaineer had won two Olympic golds in 2018 and was a celebrated skier and guide.
