Josh Tongue told England could chase India's 371 runs in the first Test. He said the batting line‑up is strong and they will stay positive. England have already made 21 runs in the first session. If they reach the target it would be the biggest chase at Headingley and the largest in England in 77 years. India bowlers will try to stop them but England aim to win.
