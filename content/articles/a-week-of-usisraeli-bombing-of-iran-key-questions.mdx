After a week of US‑Israeli bombings on Iran, the war has spread to neighboring nations and caused many civilian deaths. The attacks, called “Epic Fury” by the U.S. and “Roaring Lion” by Israel, aim to destroy Iran’s nuclear program and possibly topple its leadership. Roughly 1,200 people in Iran and dozens elsewhere have died, including children at a school. The war has closed the Hormuz Strait, disrupted oil and travel, and sparked questions about legality and regional safety.
