Police in Canada say missing Iranian mathematician Masood Masjoody was probably murdered. He was outspoken against Iran’s government and the former shah’s family. He disappeared in February in Burnaby, BC. The RCMP are still looking for his body and treating it as homicide. Activists fear Tehran may have targeted him to silence critics abroad. The case follows other reports of Iranian attacks on dissidents in Canada.
