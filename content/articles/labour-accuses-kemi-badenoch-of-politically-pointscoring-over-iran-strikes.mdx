Labour says Conservative Kemi Badenoch is trying to score political points by criticizing Labour leader Keir Starmer for being too cautious about the US‑Israel air strikes on Iran. Badenoch said Starmer avoided taking a firm stance and that Britain needs decisive leadership. The defence minister warned that using a serious security situation for political soundbites is irresponsible. Badenoch also mentioned domestic policy changes on mental health and anti‑social behaviour.
