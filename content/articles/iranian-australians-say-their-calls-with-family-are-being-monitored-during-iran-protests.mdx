After Iran shut down its internet, many Iranian Australians could only reach relatives by phone. This week, some people heard a warning on the line: “you need to hang up now.” They believe Iranian security forces are listening to calls, especially when people talk about the protests. The government’s blackout and heavy surveillance come as the country faces its biggest uprising in years, with thousands killed.
