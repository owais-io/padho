Donald Trump is still deciding whether to order airstrikes on Iran. He says the decision will rely on his advisers Steve Witkoff and Jared Kushner, who are meeting in Geneva this week to discuss a possible deal with Tehran. Trump wants a deal, but if one isn’t reached, he could consider military action. He has asked other officials for input but hasn’t made a final choice yet.
