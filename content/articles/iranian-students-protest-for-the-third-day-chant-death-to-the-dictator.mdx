University students across Iran kept protesting for a third day, calling for the end of the regime. They burned the flag, climbed trees to hang toy mice as a mock of the supreme leader, and shouted slogans. Basij forces hit a campus in Tehran, injuring students. The unrest comes as the U.S. ramps up pressure on Iran, while diplomats try to negotiate a new nuclear deal in Geneva.
