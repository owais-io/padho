A wave of AI‑made videos shows dramatic war scenes that never happened, like missiles hitting Tel Aviv or U.S. soldiers captured by Iran. Even real photos from reliable news outlets are sometimes accused of being doctored, sparking disputes. Experts say the only way to stay safe is to doubt everything at first, look for trusted fact‑checkers, and read the full context before sharing.
