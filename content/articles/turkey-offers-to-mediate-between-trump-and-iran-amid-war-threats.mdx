For ten years the United States and Iran have had no direct talks. President Trump has warned Iran that the U.S. could strike its military bases. Turkey, led by President Erdoğan, is offering to act as a middleman. Iran’s foreign minister Abbas Araghchi will go to Ankara for talks, and Turkey has suggested a video call between Trump and Iran’s president. The aim is to stop a war.
