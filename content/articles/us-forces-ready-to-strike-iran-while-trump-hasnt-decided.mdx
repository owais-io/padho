U.S. forces are ready to strike Iran within days if Trump orders it, with aircraft carriers and fighter jets already in the region. The Pentagon says it can launch an attack soon, but President Trump hasn’t decided yet and has left diplomacy as the first option. Iran is expected to submit a proposal after Geneva talks, but no deadline has been set. Officials warn that a strike could widen tensions and spark wider conflict.
