Trump aide Stephen Miller has called out India for buying oil from Russia, saying it helps fund the war in Ukraine. He warned the U.S. could sanction India, but Delhi plans to keep buying Russian oil. The comment came as Trump urged Russia to stop the fighting. Meanwhile, Ukrainian drones have hit Russian oil sites and a train station, with Russia reporting many drones intercepted.
