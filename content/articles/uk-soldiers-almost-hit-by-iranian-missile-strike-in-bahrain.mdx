UK soldiers were almost hit by an Iranian missile and drone strike near a U.S. naval base in Bahrain, putting 300 British troops within 200 metres of danger. No one was hurt, but the attack was part of a wave of Iranian strikes after the U.S‑Israel bombing campaign. British forces have been busy intercepting drones and missiles in Iraq, Qatar and elsewhere to protect allies. The UK remains on high alert in the region.
