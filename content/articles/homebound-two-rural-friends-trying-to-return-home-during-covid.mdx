Homebound is a realistic Indian film about two friends, Chandan and Shoaib, who come from a poor village in northern India. They want to move to the city to find better jobs, but their plans are delayed by paperwork and a pandemic. The movie shows their struggles, friendship, and the hardships of migration. It has strong acting, good photography, and a quiet musical score. The film premiered at Cannes and is now in UK and Irish cinemas from September 26.
