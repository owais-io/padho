Protesters in Iran marched in many cities, demanding political change after the currency fell. Supreme Leader Khamenei called them “vandals” and threatened harsher repression. The government shut down the internet, killing dozens and arresting thousands. The U.S. and European leaders urged restraint. A major protest in Tehran saw fire and chants of “death to Khamenei.” The crackdown has intensified, with reports of violence and growing unrest.
