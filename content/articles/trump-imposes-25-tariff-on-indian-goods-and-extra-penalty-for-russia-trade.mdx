Trump announced a 25% tariff on goods from India and a further penalty for buying Russian arms and energy. He points out India’s large trade gap with the U.S. and its heavy purchases from Russia amid the Ukraine war. Washington set an August 1 deadline for trade deals. The move comes as Trump pushes other nations to sign agreements, while investors watch for global market impacts.
