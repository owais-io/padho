Robert Reich says the U.S. war in Iran is a mistake. He argues the cost is huge—$1 billion a day—and most Americans oppose it. The war hurts ordinary people, raises energy prices, and drains resources that could improve health, housing, and schools. Reich calls for protests, stronger elections, and a mid‑term vote to stop the president. He wants America to fix its democracy before more damage happens.
