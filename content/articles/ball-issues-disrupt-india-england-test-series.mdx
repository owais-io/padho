The England‑India test series has been disrupted by complaints over the cricket ball’s condition. Rishabh Pant has criticised the Dukes ball, saying it wears too fast and even threw one in frustration. Dukes, a British company, is bidding for India’s official ball contract, while some Indian players are sponsored by rival supplier SG. The article says the ball’s wear is a normal part of modern red‑ball cricket, not a conspiracy.
