The Taliban have banned all Afghan women from schools and jobs, and stopped girls from attending secondary school. This makes Afghanistan the only country where girls can’t go to high school. The move is condemned as a crime against humanity. While some governments keep talking to the Taliban, this could undo the regime. The UN says girls’ education must be part of any talks.
