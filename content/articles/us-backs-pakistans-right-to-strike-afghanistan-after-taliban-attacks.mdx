The US says it supports Pakistan’s airstrikes on Afghanistan, which came after the Taliban allegedly fired on Pakistani border troops. Washington views the Taliban as a terrorist group and wants Pakistan to defend itself. Both sides have threatened more violence, but the UK, EU and Qatar urge talks to stop the fighting. The conflict could hurt civilians and destabilise the region.
