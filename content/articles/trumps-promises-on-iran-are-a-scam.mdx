Marina Hyde argues that Donald Trump’s claims he can end Iran’s nuclear program and change its regime are false. She points out that the US war on Iran is messy, that Trump’s promises about democracy are empty, and that the war could have lasting negative effects. Hyde also criticises the Trump administration’s handling of AI deals, saying the Pentagon cut ties with Anthropic for safety reasons. Overall, she says Trump’s actions harm America and the world.
