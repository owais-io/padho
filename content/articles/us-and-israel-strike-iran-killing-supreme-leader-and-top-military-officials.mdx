Iran retaliated by firing missiles and drones at Israel, US bases, and Gulf states, proving it will keep fighting. Trump said the strike could force Iranians to change the regime, while Israel aims to weaken Tehran. The attack may trigger a new leader or political split, leaving the region more unstable.
