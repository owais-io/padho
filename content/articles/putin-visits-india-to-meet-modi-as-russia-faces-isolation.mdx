Putin will visit India to meet Prime Minister Modi, marking Russia’s return to normal diplomacy after its Ukraine war isolated it. India, squeezed by U.S. tariffs and wary of China’s growing power, seeks a balance between buying Russian weapons and not becoming too dependent. The talks will cover defence deals, oil trade and strengthening ties, while India will urge peace in Ukraine but has limited influence.
