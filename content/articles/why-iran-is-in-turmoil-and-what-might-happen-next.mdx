People in Iran have been marching against the government, killing at least 648 police and putting over 10,000 people in jail. They are upset over bad food prices, power cuts and a lack of jobs. The government has beaten doctors and shut the internet. The US president, Donald Trump, has said he might use force. The Iranian regime is already weak from years of sanctions, so many wonder if it could fall.
