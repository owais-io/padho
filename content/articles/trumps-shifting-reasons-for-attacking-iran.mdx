Trump’s shifting reasons for attacking Iran have confused lawmakers. On the day he launched the strike, he called it a move against threat and a chance for Iranians to reclaim their country. Hours later he said the war could finish in a few days or end after a campaign. Other officials – the Pentagon, State Department, and the UN – offered different motives: an imminent nuclear danger, Israel’s planned attack, and Iran’s aggression. The mixed messages show no clear strategy.
