A 16‑year‑old protester in western Iran was sexually assaulted by riot police while in custody, says the Kurdistan Human Rights Network. The abuse, involving batons and physical pressure, is one of many reports of rape, beatings and deaths that have risen as the government cracks down on the protests that began in December. With a communication blackout, authorities remain silent, but human‑rights groups warn that thousands are still at risk.
