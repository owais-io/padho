A car exploded outside Delhi’s Red Fort, killing eight people and injuring about twenty. Police are looking into how the vehicle moved before the blast and have filed a case under the anti‑terror law. Officials say the attack could be terrorism, an accident, or a vehicle failure. Two people linked to the car were detained. The country is on high alert, and leaders promise to catch those responsible.
