After a week of Israel‑led airstrikes that hit Iran’s capital, Iran has begun striking back across the Gulf and even Israel. President Trump said it’s “too late” to talk to Tehran and that the US could stay in the conflict longer than a month. Iranian drones even set fire to the US embassy in Riyadh. Israel has also targeted Hezbollah forces in southern Lebanon, while oil prices rise after Iran threatens the Strait of Hormuz.
