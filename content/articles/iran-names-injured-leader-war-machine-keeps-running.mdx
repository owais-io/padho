Iran has named Mojtaba Khamenei, who was hit by Israeli rockets, as its new supreme leader. He’s broken a leg and has facial wounds, but officials say he is not in danger and can still give orders. Because no photos or speeches have come out, people are skeptical, and some say the army chose a dying man to keep control. The Revolutionary Guards run the country’s war effort automatically, so the injury doesn’t change the daily fight.
