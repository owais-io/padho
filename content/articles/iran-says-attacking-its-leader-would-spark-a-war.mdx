Iran’s president warned that any attempt to kill or harm Supreme Leader Ayatollah Ali Khamenei would trigger a full‑scale war. He blamed U.S. sanctions for the country’s economic pain and the deadly protests. Meanwhile, former U.S. President Donald Trump called for Khamenei’s resignation, and plans for a U.S. strike on Iran almost materialised. The Iranian government has shut down internet access to curb protests, while the U.S. and other nations urge restraint.
