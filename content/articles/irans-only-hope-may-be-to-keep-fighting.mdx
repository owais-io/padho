US and Israel launched a big bombing campaign on Iran, aiming to kill its leader, Ali Khamenei, and hit key military sites. Iran responded by firing missiles and drones at Israeli and U.S. targets, sparking a wider regional flare‑up. Casualties have risen on both sides, and the U.S. has brought two aircraft carriers into the area. With little chance of a ground invasion, Iran may simply try to survive the attacks.
