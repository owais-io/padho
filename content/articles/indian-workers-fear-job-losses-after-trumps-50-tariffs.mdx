Trump’s sudden 50% tariff on half of India’s exports to the U.S. has hit key industries like textiles, shoes, jewellery and shrimp. The new taxes raise costs from about 10‑15% to over 60%, making Indian goods too expensive. Factories in places like Tirupur are closing, and workers worry they could lose their jobs. The move could cut India’s GDP by up to 1% and hurt millions of families.
