Many Iranian protesters thought U.S. President Donald Trump would help them when he promised rescue if the government started shooting. A 38‑year‑old, Siavash Shirzad, joined the street march and was later shot dead. Trump later reversed, saying no military action and that executions would not happen. The crackdown continues, rights groups warn, and the Iranian diaspora feels betrayed by the empty promises. Protesters now wait for what Trump will do next.
