Pete Hegseth was confirmed as the U.S. Secretary of Defense, but many critics alarmed by his harsh comments on the Iran war and his past controversial behavior. He’s been accused of making sexist remarks, drinking on duty, and promoting Christian nationalist and extremist language. Senators split 50‑50 to confirm him, and veterans worry he may use the military for war rather than careful strategy.
