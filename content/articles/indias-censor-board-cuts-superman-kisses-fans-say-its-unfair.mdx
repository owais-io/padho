India’s censor board removed two kissing moments in the new Superman movie, saying the 33‑second embrace was too sensual for Indian audiences. Fans complained that the cuts look abrupt and that the board lets violence and abuse on screen while banning a normal kiss. The move highlights India’s still‑conservative view on on‑screen romance and past censorship controversies over other films.
