Iran’s elite leaders are accused of using state money to send their adult children to live in the West while the country faces economic hardship and government crackdowns. Critics say these privileged families enjoy a comfortable life abroad, unlike ordinary Iranians who struggle. The US and other countries may take action, but there is no clear policy to deal with the elite’s relatives. Public anger grows after protests that killed many.
