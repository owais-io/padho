David McCloskey, who used to work for the CIA, says Israel’s Mossad was surprised to learn that the U.S. CIA had helped gather the precise info needed to hit Iranian leaders, including Ayatollah Khamenei. He notes that Israel’s team can use Persian speakers and inside contacts better than the CIA, giving them an edge in spying on Iran. McCloskey also talks about his book and how the war still lacks a clear plan.
