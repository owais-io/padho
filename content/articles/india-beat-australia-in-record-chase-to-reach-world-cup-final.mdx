India chased Australia’s 338 to win the women’s World Cup semi‑final, making a record run chase. Jemimah Rodrigues stayed unbeaten for 127, and her partnership with Amanjot Kaur brought the last 29 runs in the final overs. India won by five wickets, earning a spot in the final against South Africa at the same venue. Australia’s strong start fell apart after a few wickets and some missed chances.
