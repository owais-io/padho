Pakistan’s government has officially put Donald Trump’s name forward for the Nobel Peace Prize after he helped broker a ceasefire between Israel and Iran. The move has stirred controversy because the U.S. recently bombed Iranian nuclear sites, a strike many say broke international law. While some Pakistani officials praise Trump’s peace work, critics—opposition lawmakers, diplomats, and activists—argue the nomination is misguided and call for its withdrawal.
