India’s women cricketers have won praise during the World Cup, but the story shows more than sports. In cities, women face harassment and feel unsafe walking alone. After Australian players were harassed in Indore, officials blamed the victims, not the attackers. Cricket gives women jobs, money and a chance to break stereotypes. With teams, coaches and fans cheering, cricket is helping women claim public space and confidence.
