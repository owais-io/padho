Ben Stokes has a serious shoulder muscle tear and won’t play the last Test against India. England will captain the side without him and will need to win to seal a 2‑1 series victory. The injury could keep him out for 6‑10 weeks, meaning he may miss the start of the Ashes in Australia. England have picked new bowlers and a new batsman, while India are missing injured vice‑captain Rishabh Pant. Both teams say they still respect each other.
