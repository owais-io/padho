The T20 World Cup’s Super 8s are in full swing. India is the favourite, scoring the most runs and winning the most games. Pakistan did well but fell apart against India. Sri Lanka beat Australia but lost to Zimbabwe. England have mixed results; Sam Curran has been a bright spot with runs and wickets. New Zealand, West Indies, South Africa and Zimbabwe all have chances, but India stays on top.
