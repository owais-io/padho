The US and Iran are preparing for another round of talks in Geneva this week, hoping to reduce Iran’s stock of highly enriched uranium and prove it isn’t seeking a nuclear weapon. Meanwhile protests are still happening in Tehran and Mashhad, with students clashing with security forces. Iran says it will let the IAEA monitor its sites and lower its uranium levels in return for easing sanctions. Trump’s team is pushing for a deal that could replace the 2015 agreement.
