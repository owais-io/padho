Tehran has been hit by constant airstrikes since the US and Israel launched attacks last month, killing more than 1,200 people, including Iran’s top leader, Ali Khamenei. Residents are forced to flee their homes after bombs destroy apartment blocks and hit schools, leaving thousands injured. Despite the war, markets and some restaurants stay open, but traffic is dangerous and the city feels unsafe. Iranian officials say they will defend themselves, and the fighting spreads across the region.
