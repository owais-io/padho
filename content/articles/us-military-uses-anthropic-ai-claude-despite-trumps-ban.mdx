Donald Trump ordered all U.S. government agencies to stop using the AI system Claude from Anthropic, but the military still used it to plan and carry out an attack on Iran. The AI helped with intelligence, target selection, and simulations. Trump’s move sparked a clash with the Pentagon, which says it can’t quickly drop the tech because it’s already built into many operations. After the split, OpenAI’s tools are now being offered to the military.
