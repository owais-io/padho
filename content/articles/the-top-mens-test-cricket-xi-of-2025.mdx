The Guardian’s yearly list picked the best 13 male Test players based on their 2025 performances. Travis Head tops the chart with 759 runs, while Shubman Gill, Joe Root, and Alex Carey also shine. Highlights include Gill’s 983 runs as India’s new captain, Root’s 790 runs, and Starc’s 51 wickets. The list shows who stood out in a year full of big scores, key wickets, and memorable moments.
