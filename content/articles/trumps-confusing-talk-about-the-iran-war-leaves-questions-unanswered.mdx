One week into a new US‑Iran war, President Trump gave unclear answers about what victory looks like and how long the conflict will last. He said he wants Iran to surrender unconditionally but didn’t explain what that means. Trump even claimed a deadly school strike was carried out by Iran, a claim his advisers disputed. He says the war will continue, and that casualties are “part of war.” The mixed messages leave many questions still open.
