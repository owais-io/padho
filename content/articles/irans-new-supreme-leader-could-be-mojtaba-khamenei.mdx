After the death of Iran’s former Supreme Leader Ali Khamenei, his son Mojtaba is seen by many as the likely successor. The IRGC says he’s their pick, and the Assembly of Experts – the group that chooses the leader – is close to voting. No official announcement has been made yet, and the decision may wait until after the funeral. Israel even struck a building in Qom linked to the selection process.
