Rafia Afi started India’s first divorce camp in Kerala, inviting 15‑20 women for two‑day hikes and support sessions. The retreats mix nature walks, music, storytelling and legal talks to help ex‑spouses rebuild confidence and learn their rights. Many say the camp ends shame, turns trauma into strength, and gives a safe space to share experiences. Afi plans more camps in big cities and online content.
