The Trump administration has offered several shifting explanations for the U.S. attack on Iran. Some point to Iran’s crackdown on protestors, others to its nuclear or missile programs, and the latest claim is that Iran is about to strike first. A congressional source, Marco Rubio, said the real trigger was Israel’s plan to hit Iran first, forcing U.S. forces into a fight. Critics argue the government has misled the public and that Israeli Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu helped push the war.
