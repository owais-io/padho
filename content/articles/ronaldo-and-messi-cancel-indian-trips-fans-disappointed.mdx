Ronaldo and Messi both pulled out of planned games in India, hurting fans who had been excited to see them. Ronaldo missed a big AFC Champions League match in Goa, where fans and clubs had made special arrangements. Messi was set to play a friendly with Australia in Kerala, but the match was cancelled after FIFA refused approval. These cancellations add to a tough season for Indian football, which is already dealing with league delays and national team setbacks.
