Trump criticized NATO, saying allied soldiers stayed a bit back during the Afghanistan war and questioning if they would defend the U.S. He pointed out that most U.S. troops died there, and that NATO invoked Article 5 only after 9/11. NATO leaders and veterans responded, pointing out the sacrifices made and assuring support. The comments stirred debate on the U.S.’s reliance on its allies.
