US and Israel bombing has damaged many old buildings in Iran, including the 14th‑century Golestan Palace in Tehran and the 17th‑century Chehel Sotoon Palace in Isfahan. The blasts shattered mirrors and broke windows, but the main walls stayed mostly intact. Iranian officials say the attacks violate international rules that protect heritage. UNESCO and other groups have warned the damage is irreversible.
