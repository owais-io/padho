After a wave of missiles and airstrikes by the U.S. and Israel, Iranian state media says the country’s top leader, Supreme Leader Ali Khamenei, was killed. The attacks also hit other Iranian officials, the Revolutionary Guard, and U.S. bases in the Gulf. Israel and the U.S. say the goal was to force a regime change, while Iran vows retaliation. The strike has triggered a wider regional crisis with many civilians hurt.
