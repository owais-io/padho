Jofra Archer is ready to come back to the England Test team for the third match against India at Lord's. His coach wants the pitch to give pace and bounce to help him bowl well. Archer missed the previous game after a serious injury and has played only a few matches in recent years. England’s fast bowlers will need rest, and Archer’s return could give the team a boost.
