Two trans rights activists in Karachi were shot at home in January, but only one was hit and both escaped. The attack is part of a wave of violence against trans people in Pakistan, with 55 killings recorded in Sindh alone since 2022. While activists file complaints and push for protection, local authorities sometimes pressure trans people to leave their homes. The community is rallying for legal safeguards and better enforcement of existing rights laws.
