Australia has dropped wicket‑keeper batsman Marnus Labuschagne from its upcoming one‑day series against India after two early dismissals in recent matches. Queensland teammate Matt Renshaw has been named in his place. The squad also includes returning fast bowler Mitchell Starc and fast‑batsman Mitch Owen, who may debut after a concussion. Pat Cummins is resting from a back injury, while captain Mitchell Marsh will lead the side.
