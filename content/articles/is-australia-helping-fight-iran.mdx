Australia is drawn into the U.S. and Israel’s strikes on Iran, supplying weapons, intelligence and training. Officials say it’s only “defensive” help, but critics argue Australia is a co‑belligerent. The government also sent aircraft and missiles to the UAE, a key weapons customer. The debate centers on whether Australia is protecting its own people or acting as an ally in an illegal war.
