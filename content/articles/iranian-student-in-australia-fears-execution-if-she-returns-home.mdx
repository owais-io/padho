An Iranian woman who studied in Australia says she could be executed if she goes back to Iran after her student visa ends. She joined protests in Tehran in 2022 and has been targeted by the government. Now she has only a few weeks left on her visa, but because of new age limits she can’t apply for a post‑study visa. Australia is watching the situation, but many experts say the country needs clearer rules to help people in danger.
