In Tehran and other cities, thousands joined protests after the cost of living rose. Police fired on the crowds and the government cut the internet to stop news. Over 500 people were killed, and more than 10,000 were arrested. People shouted against the ruler and for the former royal family. The United States threatened to intervene, and activists abroad shared videos that showed the violence.
