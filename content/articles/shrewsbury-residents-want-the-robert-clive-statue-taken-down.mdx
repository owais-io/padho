A letter from Shrewsbury's Jean Garner Minsterley says the town is unhappy with the statue of Robert Clive on its main street. After a 9,000‑signature petition, the council kept the statue and added a plaque after three years. Residents feel the figure, called an “unstable sociopath” by William Dalrymple, makes them uncomfortable. They hope political support will lead to the statue’s removal.
