India and England ended their Test series tied after a dramatic 5th match at the Oval. With England needing 35 runs to finish 374, they collapsed in a noisy crowd, while Indian fast bowler Mohammed Siraj took five wickets to seal the win. England’s Chris Woakes was sidelined by injury and didn’t bowl. The match finished six runs in India’s favour, giving both teams a 2‑2 series share.
