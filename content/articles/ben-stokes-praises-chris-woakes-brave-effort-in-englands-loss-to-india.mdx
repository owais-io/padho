Ben Stokes praised the bravery of Chris Woakes, who played with a broken shoulder in England’s seven‑run loss to India. Stokes said the team was nearly able to win and that Woakes’ injury changed the game. He also noted the impact of six dropped catches and how teammates stepped up after the injury. Stokes praised the courage shown and said the series taught players more about themselves.
