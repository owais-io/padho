UK factories have seen a sharp drop in orders, leaving manufacturers worried about high energy costs and low demand. At the same time, oil prices have jumped to over $70 a barrel after reports that the U.S. may strike Iran, raising worries about inflation and interest rates. The British government is being asked for help, while the British Gas owner Centrica has cut its profits and paused share buybacks.
