British residents in Dubai have been shaken by Iranian missile attacks that hit the city, killing and injuring people. Despite the danger, many Britons say life is “business as usual”. Government warnings about sharing unverified videos and internet outages added to concerns. The UK government has advised British nationals to register and stay safe. Some businesses and schools are closed, but many workers return to work. Experts think the tourism and economy will recover in a year or two.
