India fought back to secure a draw in the fourth Test, keeping their series hopes alive. After England’s Ben Stokes gave his best all‑round effort, India’s batting stormled on the final day. Ravindra Jadeja and Washington Sundar finished unbeaten on 107 and 101, while Shubman Gill added another century. England now faces a must‑win match at the Oval to stay in the hunt.
