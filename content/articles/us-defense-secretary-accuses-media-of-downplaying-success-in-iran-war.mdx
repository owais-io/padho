Pete Hegseth said the U.S. and Israel have knocked out Iran’s air force, navy, and missile sites in 13 days, and called the press “downplaying” the gains. He also claimed Iranian leaders are hiding, even as a parade shows they’re still active. Despite the attacks, Iran keeps launching missiles and drones, and the U.S. is still investigating a strike on an Iranian girls’ school that killed 175 people. Hegseth also criticized coverage of the Strait of Hormuz and even named CNN in his rant.
