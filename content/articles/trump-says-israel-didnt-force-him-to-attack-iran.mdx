Trump said he didn’t attack Iran because Israel pushed him. He told reporters that he saw Iran as a threat and that the U.S. had to act before Iran attacked first. The comments came after Senator Marco Rubio said the strikes were to stop Iranian retaliation. Democrats were furious and accused Trump of lying. The story also notes that Trump has been mixed on sending troops to the Middle East.
