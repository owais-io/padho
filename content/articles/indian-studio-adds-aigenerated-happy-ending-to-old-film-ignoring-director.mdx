A Mumbai‑based studio is re‑releasing the 2013 romance *Raanjhanaa* under a new title, *Ambikapathy*, but with an AI‑made happy ending that replaces the original tragedy. The change was made without the director’s consent, sparking anger and possible legal action. The studio says it’s experimenting with AI to refresh old movies, while the director argues it strips filmmakers of control. The move highlights growing debates over AI in cinema.
