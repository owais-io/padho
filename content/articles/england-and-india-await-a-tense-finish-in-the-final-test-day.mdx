England had built a lead in the fifth Test, powered by centuries from Harry Brook and Joe Root. After rain, India fought back, taking six wickets and leaving England just 35 runs short. The game was paused at 6 pm with the match still in doubt. England needs a win to finish 3–1, but India can still draw level and share the trophy. Key moments include Brook’s century and Root’s dismissal.
