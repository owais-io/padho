India is celebrating 80 years of independence while a summit in Delhi sees the country looking to use AI from US tech giants to boost its economy. Prime Minister Narendra Modi talked about AI as a game‑changer, and big names like OpenAI, Google and Anthropic announced deals to bring their chatbots to India. The US government supports the move, warning of a Chinese threat, while India weighs the trade‑off between US and Chinese AI models. Concerns remain about control and cultural impact.
