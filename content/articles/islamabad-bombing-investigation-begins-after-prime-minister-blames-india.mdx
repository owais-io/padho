A suicide bombing outside Islamabad’s district courts killed 12 and injured 27. Pakistan opened an investigation after Prime Minister Shehbaz Sharif accused India‑backed Afghan militants, but India denied any involvement. The attack was first claimed by the Taliban group TTP, which later said it wasn’t responsible. The incident follows a recent blast in Delhi and highlights rising terror threats and tense India‑Pakistan relations.
