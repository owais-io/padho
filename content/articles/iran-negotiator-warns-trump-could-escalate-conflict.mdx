US special envoy Robert Malley says the recent US‑Israeli strikes on Iran and Iran’s attacks on Gulf shipping could spark more conflict. He explains that the stalled JCPOA and Trump’s unpredictable decision‑making keep the situation open, giving Trump a potential exit but also risking a longer, costlier war. Malley argues that Iran’s nuclear program may still recover quickly, and the fallout could haunt the region for decades.
