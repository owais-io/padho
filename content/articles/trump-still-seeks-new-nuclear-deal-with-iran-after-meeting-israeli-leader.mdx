President Donald Trump said he is still trying to negotiate a new deal with Iran that would curb its nuclear program. He met with Israeli Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu for three hours, but no agreement was reached. Trump said negotiations should continue and that if a deal is reached he would let Netanyahu know. He also warned that if Iran refuses, the US might use force again. Israel wants Iran to limit missiles and cut support for groups like Hamas.
