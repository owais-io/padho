England’s women tied the ODI series with India after a rain‑shortened game at Lord’s. The match dropped to 29 overs per side, but England still chased 115. Sophie Dunkley hit the final boundary, and captain Amy Jones guided a smooth chase. The game had a few drama moments – India’s earlier run‑out of Charlie Dean and a disputed obstruction call involving Tammy Beaumont – but England finished comfortably. The series decider will be played in Durham.
