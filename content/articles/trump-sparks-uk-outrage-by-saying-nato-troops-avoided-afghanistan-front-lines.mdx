Trump claimed NATO stayed off the front lines in Afghanistan, sparking anger from UK MPs and veterans. Critics point out the 457 British deaths and the 3,486 total NATO casualties, noting Trump’s history of dodging Vietnam duty. Politicians across the spectrum called the remarks insulting and inaccurate, urging him to apologize for disrespecting the sacrifice of soldiers.
