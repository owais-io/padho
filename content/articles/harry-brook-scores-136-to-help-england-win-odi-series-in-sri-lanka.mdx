Harry Brook's blazing 136 off 66 balls helped England win the final ODI in Sri Lanka, taking a 1‑0 series lead. Brook hit nine sixes and 11 fours as England chased a massive 358 target. Sri Lanka's Pavan Rathnayake made a century, but wickets fell early. England's win was their first ODI series victory abroad in three years, ending Sri Lanka's home winning streak.
