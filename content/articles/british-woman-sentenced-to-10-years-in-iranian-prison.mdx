Lindsay Foreman and her husband were jailed in Tehran on espionage charges after being arrested while traveling to Australia. Lindsay, who pleaded her innocence, says she has endured harsh conditions and a hunger strike in Evin prison. The British government calls the sentence “unjust” and is pressing for their release, but no clear path has emerged. The case highlights the danger foreign nationals face in Iran.
