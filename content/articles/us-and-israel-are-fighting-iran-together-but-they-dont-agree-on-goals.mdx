The United States and Israel are fighting Iran together for the first time, but they don’t agree on what they want. The war is closely coordinated, yet the U.S. wants a quick, low‑cost win while Israel seeks to topple Iran’s regime. The clash has raised oil prices, hurt U.S. allies, and left Gulf states uneasy. With no clear endgame, the war risks becoming a costly failure.
