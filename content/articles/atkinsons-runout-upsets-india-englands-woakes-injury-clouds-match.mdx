In the Oval Test, England’s new fast bowler Gus Atkinson stole the spotlight by taking Shubman Gill out for 21 in a quick run‑out, cutting off India’s hopes early. Meanwhile, England’s key bowler Chris Woakes broke his shoulder, leaving the team short of a seam attack. England’s captain Ollie Pope faces a tough match with only a few bowlers left, while India must keep their momentum against a shaky English side.
