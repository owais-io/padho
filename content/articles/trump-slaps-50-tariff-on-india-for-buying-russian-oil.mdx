Trump raised U.S. duties on most Indian goods to 50% to punish India for buying cheap Russian oil. The move could hurt India’s economy and U.S.‑Indian trade, with some products like smartphones temporarily exempt. Indian leaders say they will keep buying Russian oil and encourage buying local goods. The tariffs have rattled markets, made Indian exports less competitive, and pushed India closer to Russia and China.
