The novel follows Ma, her husband, a toddler, and her father as they try to get climate visas to the U.S. While floods and heat make Kolkata a food‑scarce city, Ma secretly steals food from a shelter to survive. When a stranger named Boomba steals their passports, the family’s plans crumble. The review notes the book’s fast pacing and moral questions but criticises its thin plot and unrealistic details.
