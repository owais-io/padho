At a March 4 briefing, Pentagon officials answered questions from right‑wing media about a June operation in Iran. Reporters asked why the US is re‑engaging, what intelligence shows Iran is a new threat, and if Israel is taking advantage. Some journalists praised the questioning, while others said it was weak or propaganda. The Pentagon now lets some legacy reporters back in, but limits camera use and keeps the room tightly controlled.
