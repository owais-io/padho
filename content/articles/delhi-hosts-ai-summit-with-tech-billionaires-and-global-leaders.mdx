Tech giants like Google, OpenAI, and Anthropic will travel to Delhi for an AI summit hosted by Prime Minister Narendra Modi. The week‑long event aims to show India as a new AI centre for the global south and to discuss how the technology can help farmers, water supplies, and health services. Leaders from Africa, Asia, and Europe will also attend. The summit will look at AI safety, fairness and how it can reach poorer people.
