Liberal MP Alex Hawke has asked Senator Jacinta Nampijinpa Price to apologise after she said the federal migration plan favoured some countries, naming Indians as an example. The remarks angered the Indian community, and the party has been trying to mend the damage. Price hasn’t formally apologised, and party leaders have not stepped in either. Hawke says a quick apology would fix the problem and restore trust.
