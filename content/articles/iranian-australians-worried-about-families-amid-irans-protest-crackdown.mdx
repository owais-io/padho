A wave of protests in Iran has led the government to shut down internet and cut off international calls. Many Iranian Australians are worried because they cannot confirm if their loved ones are safe. Some have received short calls that end quickly, while others have no news at all. The crackdown has caused thousands of deaths, and families of protesters are scared. Activists in Australia keep hoping for help and urging the world to act.
