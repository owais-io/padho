The United States announced a multi‑day air‑drill in the Middle East as tensions with Iran rise. The exercise will feature the USS Abraham Lincoln aircraft carrier, fighter jets and support ships to show U.S. power and test rapid deployment. It also comes after a surge of U.S. forces, including UK jets, to the region. The drills are a warning while Washington considers strikes over Iran’s violent crackdown on protestors. Allies warn the U.S. should respect local airspace.
