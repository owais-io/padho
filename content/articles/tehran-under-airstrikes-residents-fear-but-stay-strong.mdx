In Tehran, American and Israeli airstrikes have hit the city, leaving residents scared and many staying at home. The internet is blocked, so people rely on phones to share what they see. Schools are closed but hospitals stay open. Despite the danger, people still go to markets and even jog. After news of the Iranian leader’s death, there were brief celebrations, but police quickly shut them down. Overall, fear is high, but some hope remains.
