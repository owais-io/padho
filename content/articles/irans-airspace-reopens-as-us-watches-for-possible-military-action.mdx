Iran’s airspace was closed for five hours before reopening at 3 a.m. Thursday after worries that U.S. forces might strike. President Trump said there is no plan to execute protesters, though U.S. officials are monitoring the situation closely. Iran has killed 2,571 people in a crackdown, with over 18,000 arrested. The U.S., U.K., and other governments urged their citizens to leave, and the G7 is ready to impose extra sanctions.
