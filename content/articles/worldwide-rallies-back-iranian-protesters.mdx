People in cities around the world gathered to support Iranians who are protesting against their government. In Munich, ex‑king’s son Reza Pahlavi urged the crowd to keep chanting and promised he could lead a democratic change. Similar rallies happened in Los Angeles, London, Toronto, and more. The U.S. has warned Iran and even talked about military help, while rights groups say the crackdown has killed thousands and jailed over 50,000. Talks on Iran’s nuclear programme are still underway.
