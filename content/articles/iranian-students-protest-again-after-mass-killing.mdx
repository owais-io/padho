After a brutal January crackdown that killed thousands, students across Iranian universities are marching, chanting anti‑government slogans, and refusing to attend class. They say the emptiness of classrooms is because graves are full. Students clash with Basij security forces and vow to risk their lives for future freedom, demanding the regime end its violence and stop labeling dissenters as foreign agents.
