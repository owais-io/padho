President Trump blasted Iran's leaders as “deranged scumbags” and warned the U.S. would strike hard. U.S. and Israeli planes bombed targets in Tehran, including near a university square, while Iran fired missiles and drones at Israel, Lebanon and Gulf states. The fighting has caused dozens of deaths, wounded many, and raised fears about oil shipping through the Strait of Hormuz. The situation keeps growing tense across the region.
