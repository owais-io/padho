Imran Khan, former Pakistan prime minister, has been in jail since 2023 and is said to have a serious eye problem. His party says he’s kept alone and doctors can’t see him for months. The eye issue, a blocked retinal vein, can cause permanent blindness if untreated. Lawyers and family protested, but the jail used tear gas and refuses visits, violating his rights.
