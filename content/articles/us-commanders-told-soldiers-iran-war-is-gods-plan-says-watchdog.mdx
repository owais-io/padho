A watchdog says more than 200 US service members have complained that commanders are using biblical “end‑times” language to justify fighting Iran. One NCO said the commander urged troops that the war is “part of God’s divine plan,” citing Revelation and claiming President Trump was anointed to trigger Armageddon. The complaints point to a rise in Christian nationalist rhetoric in the armed forces and question the separation of church and state.
