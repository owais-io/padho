Iranian exiles abroad give mixed views on protests, calling for change but many think it will take time and foreign help is risky. Reza Pahlavi, son of the former shah, urges the West to remove Iran’s leader, while some refugees say Iranians must take back power themselves. Others fear foreign intervention could spark war and that a new government may not be ready. The unrest has caused thousands of deaths, and families abroad struggle to stay in touch.
