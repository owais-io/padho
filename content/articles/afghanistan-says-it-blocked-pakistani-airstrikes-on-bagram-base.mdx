Afghanistan says it stopped Pakistani jets from bombing Bagram airbase in a clash that’s now in its fourth day. The Afghan police say the jets tried to hit the former US base but were repelled by air‑defence guns. Pakistan claims the fighting is part of an “open war.” Talks with Saudi Arabia and Qatar have stalled, while the world worries about militant groups in the region. Both sides report heavy losses, but the exact numbers are unclear.
