Iran and U.S. officials met in Geneva to try to reach a nuclear agreement. Iran says the deal can work if the U.S. lets it enrich uranium, lets it lower its stock of highly enriched uranium, and puts no limits on its missile program. U.S. negotiators are still figuring out if they will accept those demands. President Trump’s stance on the talks is unclear, and the U.S. Secretary of State warned that missing a missile deal could cause problems.
