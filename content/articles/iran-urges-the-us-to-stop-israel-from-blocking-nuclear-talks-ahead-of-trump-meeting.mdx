Iran warns the United States that Israel, led by Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu, could use a last‑minute meeting with President Donald Trump to derail talks about Tehran’s nuclear program. Netanyahu wants a deal that also limits Iran’s missiles, cuts support for proxy groups, and addresses human rights. Iran insists the US should not let Israel influence the negotiations. The tension comes as Iran faces internal protests and the US keeps a strong naval presence near Iran.
