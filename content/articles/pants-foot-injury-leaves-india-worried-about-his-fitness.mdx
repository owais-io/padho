India is worried about Rishabh Pant after he broke his foot on the first day of the fourth Test against England. Pant, who is the team's top scorer in the series, was out after a sharp reverse‑sweep hit his boot. The team has sent him for scans and hopes to know his condition by tomorrow. England can’t rely on him to finish the match. Pant’s injury could change England’s plan to keep them at bay.
