After the death of Iran’s top leader, Ayatollah Khamenei, and recent bomb attacks on Tehran, the country’s football future is uncertain. The federation had already struggled with coaching changes and limited support for the sport. With the World Cup approaching, officials say Iran may not be able to travel to play, and another Asian team could replace them if they withdraw. Fans and players worry about safety and the country’s chances on the world stage.
