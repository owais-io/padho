India’s Prime Minister Narendra Modi and China’s Foreign Minister Wang Yi met in Delhi, agreeing to restart flights, issue journalist visas, and boost trade and cultural ties. They also talked about easing the long‑standing Himalayan border dispute and border patrols. China plans a big dam in Tibet that worries India. Meanwhile, the US is raising tariffs on Indian goods, adding pressure. India is also keeping up oil deals with Russia and preparing for a trip to Tianjin in August.
