The US‑Israel attack on Iran shut airspace across the Middle East, grounding flights that normally stop in Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Doha and other hubs. About 115,000 Australians are stranded and many booked for Europe or elsewhere face cancellations. Emirates, Etihad and Virgin Australia offer refunds or free changes, but airlines set the terms. Passengers should contact their carrier, avoid early cancellations, and note that most travel insurance does not cover war.
