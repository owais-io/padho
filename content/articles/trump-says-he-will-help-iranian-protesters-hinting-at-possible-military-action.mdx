Trump posted on Truth Social urging Iranian protesters to keep fighting and promised “help is on its way.” He canceled all meetings with Iranian officials and warned the U.S. state department that Americans should leave Iran immediately. His words suggest he’s ready for more than economic pressure, possibly military action, if Tehran continues to crack down on demonstrators. The move could spark stronger U.S. involvement in Iran.
