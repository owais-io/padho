Iran is facing protests over killings. The government says it will publish names of the dead, but critics say it is not transparent. Reformists want an independent investigation, even from the UN. Many believe the real death toll is far higher than the official 3,117. Families fear retaliation. Tens of thousands are detained, including children, and the public wants a true, honest report.
