England were on the brink of a 374‑run chase when Mohammed Siraj knocked out Zak Crawley with a yorker, ending the match and sealing a 2‑2 series draw. With Chris Woakes injured and only nine wickets in hand, England could have lost in a single dismissal. India’s strong batting, led by Shubman Gill and Yashasvi Jaiswal’s 118, kept the pressure, giving India the series win.
