India won the Women’s Cricket World Cup opener against Sri Lanka in Guwahati, beating them by 59 runs in a rain‑affected match. Deepti Sharma was the star, scoring 53 runs and taking 3 wickets for 54. Along with Amanjot Kaur’s 57, they added a 103‑run partnership to rescue India from a shaky start. Sri Lanka were bowled out for 211. Sharma’s performance puts her close to the top of India’s all‑time ODI wicket‑takers.
