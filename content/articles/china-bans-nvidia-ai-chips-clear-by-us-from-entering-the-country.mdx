Chinese customs officials halted shipments of Nvidia’s H200 AI chips, even though the U.S. had approved their export. Suppliers stopped production, and government officials warned local tech firms not to buy the chips unless absolutely necessary. The ban’s length is unclear, and it comes amid a tense U.S.-China tech rivalry. The move could hurt Chinese firms that were counting on the chips and may push the country to develop its own chips.
