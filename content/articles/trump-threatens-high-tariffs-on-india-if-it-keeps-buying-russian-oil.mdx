Trump says if India continues buying Russian oil, the U.S. will raise tariffs on Indian goods. He has not cut duties for India as he did for other countries, and wants the price to drop. Indian leaders, led by Prime Minister Modi, have defended their trade with Russia, citing non‑alignment. The move forces Modi to choose between losing face or risking a blow‑up in trade.
