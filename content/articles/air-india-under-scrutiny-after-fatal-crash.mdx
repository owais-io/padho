A deadly crash on 12 June of Air India Flight 171 killed 241 people on board and 19 on the ground. Early findings say the plane’s fuel switches were turned off after take‑off, causing the crash. India’s aviation watchdog has sent Air India four notices about 29 safety breaches, including crew fatigue and training lapses. The airline, bought by the Tata Group, is under scrutiny as it works to fix its safety record and regain customer trust.
