A protester climbed the balcony of the Iranian embassy in London, tore down the national flag, and replaced it with the pre‑Islamic revolution lion‑and‑sun flag. The act was captured on video and shared online. Police said 500–1,000 people attended; two arrests were made for trespass and assault. The incident is part of a worldwide protest against Iran’s regime, which has seen many demonstrations and a harsh crackdown, with the UK urging Tehran to respect peaceful protest.
