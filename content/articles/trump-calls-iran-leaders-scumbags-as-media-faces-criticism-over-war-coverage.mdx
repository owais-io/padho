U.S. Defence Secretary Pete Hegseth used a Pentagon press‑conference to attack journalists for downplaying the success of the U.S.–Israeli air campaign against Iran. He suggested headline slogans like “Iran increasingly desperate” and claimed Iran’s air force, navy and missile defences were gone after 13 days of strikes. Meanwhile, President Trump warned Iran would be hit hard and branded its leaders “deranged scumbags.”
