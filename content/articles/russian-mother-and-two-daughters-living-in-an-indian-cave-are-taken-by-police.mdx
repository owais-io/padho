Police in Karnataka, India, found a 40‑year‑old Russian woman and her six‑ and four‑year‑old daughters sleeping in a damp cave. They said they moved there to meditate and escape city life, cooking simple meals and bathing in waterfalls. The family had no valid visas, having overstayed in India. Authorities removed them to a shelter and will deport the mother to Russia. Police warned the cave was risky during monsoon.
