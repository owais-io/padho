England won the second one‑day match against Sri Lanka, breaking a long losing streak at home. Joe Root scored 75, while all‑rounder Adil Rashid took 2 for 34 on a turning pitch. Sri Lanka were all out for 219, and England chased it comfortably, winning by five wickets. The victory gives England confidence after a tough few years, and Coach Brendon McCullum celebrated the win abroad.
