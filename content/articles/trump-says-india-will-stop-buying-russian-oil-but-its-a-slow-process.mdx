Trump said India will stop buying Russian oil, a move that would help cut funding for Russia’s war in Ukraine. He said the change won’t happen overnight but will happen soon. India has increased Russian oil imports to about 40% because of cheap prices under sanctions. Trump has also slapped tariffs on Indian goods, blaming the oil imports for the cost of the war.
