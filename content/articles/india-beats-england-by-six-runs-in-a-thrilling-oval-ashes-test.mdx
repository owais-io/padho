India beat England by six runs in a dramatic Oval Ashes test. The match saw India chase a small target, with bowler Mohammad Siraj leading the way. England tried to play a fast‑paced “Bazball” style, but India’s batters, led by Dinesh Karthik, steadied the chase. The game was full of tension, surprising turns and lots of crowd energy, ending with India securing a thrilling victory.
