England have won four of five matches and are almost sure of a spot in the World Cup semi‑finals. Their biggest test is tomorrow against Pakistan, a team that relies heavily on spin bowling. England’s players, especially the batsmen, have struggled against spin so far. The match will decide if they can overcome this weakness and keep their semi‑final hopes alive.
