Bangladesh’s anti‑corruption court wants Interpol to issue a red notice for British Labour MP Tulip Siddiq. The claim says Siddiq helped her aunt, former prime minister Sheikh Hasina, get a state land plot in Dhaka’s Gulshan area for a private firm. Siddiq denies the allegations, calling them political. The UK has no extradition treaty with Bangladesh, so the notice would be symbolic, not an automatic arrest. British lawyers have criticised Bangladesh’s trial process for lacking fair legal rights.
