Airlines are scrambling to help travelers stuck after the Iran war shut Dubai, Doha and other airports. Experts say airlines have teams that fix such problems, but the scale is huge. Flights are being rerouted, small planes added, and airlines like Emirates and Qantas are moving people out, but crews and planes are short. The cost is high, and the process could take weeks, leaving many passengers delayed and hotels filled.
