In 1980 my grandfather received a warning that the Iranian government wanted him dead. He, my grandmother and my teen mother fled Iran with a forged passport, later moving to Australia after years in London. The author now worries that Trump’s plan to bomb Iran could kill innocent people. He stresses civilians should never be targeted, whether by the Iranian regime or foreign forces, and calls for peace.
