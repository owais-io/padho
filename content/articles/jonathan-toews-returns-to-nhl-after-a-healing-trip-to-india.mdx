Jonathan Toews, a former Chicago Blackhawks captain, left the NHL after health problems and spent two months in India detoxing with Ayurveda. He used herbal treatments, yoga and a strict diet to clear toxins and ease his chronic inflammation. After the cleanse, he signed with the Winnipeg Jets and played his first NHL game in 2½ years. He’s back on the ice, playing well and feeling lighter and more relaxed.
