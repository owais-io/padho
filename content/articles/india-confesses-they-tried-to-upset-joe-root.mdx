India admitted it used small remarks and gestures to try to upset England captain Joe Root during the series. Former England captains Michael Vaughan and Alastair Cook said the tactic worked, and Root, who had scored two centuries, was dismissed for 29. India’s bowler Prasidh Krishna said it was just banter, while a teammate’s walk‑off gesture caused mild tension. The teams remain friendly overall.
