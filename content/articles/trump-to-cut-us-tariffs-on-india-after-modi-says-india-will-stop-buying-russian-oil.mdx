Trump said India would stop buying Russian oil after a call with Prime Minister Modi, and announced the U.S. will lower tariffs on Indian goods from 25% to 18% and drop an extra 25% tariff. He also claimed India will buy more U.S. energy and tech worth $500 bn. Modi posted about the tariffs but didn’t confirm the oil move. The changes could help U.S.-India trade and pressure Russia.
