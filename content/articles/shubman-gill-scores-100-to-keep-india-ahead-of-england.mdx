Shubman Gill’s 114 helped India reach 310 for five, giving them a solid lead after a shaky start. England, led by Ben Stokes, chose to bowl first hoping the pitch stayed consistent. The Indians added lower‑order all‑rounders like Ravindra Jadeja to steady the innings, while key bowlers such as Jasprit Bumrah were rested. England’s bowlers, especially Chris Woakes, struggled to break India’s rhythm, keeping the match in India’s favour.
