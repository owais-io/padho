Israel launched a series of airstrikes on the southern suburbs of Beirut after Hezbollah fired missiles and drones at Israel. The attacks, aimed at Hezbollah sites, left at least 31 people dead and forced thousands to evacuate. Israel also mobilised 100,000 reservists along the Lebanon border. The Lebanese government condemned the missile launch and warned the country could get drawn into a new war. The incident follows the death of Iran’s supreme leader.
