Prime Minister Keir Starmer said Britain will bring Ukrainian experts to help Gulf allies shoot down Iranian drones that have been attacking them after US‑Israeli strikes on Iran. Britain won’t join the strikes but will keep defending the region. Russia has increased missile and drone attacks on Ukraine. Ukraine’s President Zelenskyy thanked Belgium for seizing a tanker that was moving Russian oil illegally. In Russia, drone debris hit a house and a football match was postponed due to missile alerts.
