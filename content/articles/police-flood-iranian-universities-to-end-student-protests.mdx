Plain‑clothes police flooded Iranian universities to stop a fourth day of student protests against the supreme leader. On campuses, students clashed with Basij militia and saw trucks with machine‑guns parked outside. Universities shut in‑person classes, with most students now learning online. Protesters chanted slogans demanding political prisoners be freed. The crackdown comes as Iran and the U.S. prepare talks over the nuclear deal.
