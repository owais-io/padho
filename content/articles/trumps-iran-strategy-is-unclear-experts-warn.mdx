President Trump has ordered strikes on Iran and said he wants to stop the country’s nuclear program, but he hasn’t shared a clear plan for what comes next. Analysts fear the U.S. could get stuck in a long‑term war if there’s no defined goal, like regime change or peace talks. Critics say any attack must have an end strategy; without it, the U.S. could keep fighting for years.
