Jofra Archer returned to England’s Test team after a four‑year break and took five wickets for 107 runs at Lord’s, sparking a 2‑1 series win over India. His fast, accurate bowling reminded critics he still has top‑class pace. Despite past injuries and doubts about his place in the squad, Archer’s performance shows he can play at the highest level again and earn a spot in future Tests, including the Ashes.
