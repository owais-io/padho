A shocking video showing a couple being shot in Balochistan sparked anger across Pakistan. Authorities identified the victims, Bano Bibi and her groom Ahsan Ullah, and arrested 13 people connected to the killing. The attack was ordered by a tribal elder after the bride married without his approval. Human Rights groups say the country sees hundreds of “honour” killings each year, mostly against women, and want stronger action to stop them.
