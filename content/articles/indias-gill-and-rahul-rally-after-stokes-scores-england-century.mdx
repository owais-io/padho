England's Ben Stokes hit a 141‑run century and took five wickets at Old Trafford, leaving India trailing by 311 runs. But India's top batsmen, Shubman Gill and KL Rahul, steadied the innings, scoring 78 and 87* to lift the score to 174 for 2. Still 137 runs behind, rain on Sunday could force a draw. Stokes’ injury worries England, while India’s bowlers also struck, with fast bowlers taking wickets, giving hope for a comeback.
