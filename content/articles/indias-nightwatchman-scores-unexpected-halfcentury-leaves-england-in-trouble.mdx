Akash Deep, usually a bowler for England, was sent in as a nightwatchman against India at the Oval. He struck 8 sixes in 18 balls for a quick 53, then stayed on and added 66 in a long innings. His effort helped India’s Yashasvi Jaiswal also reach a century, and it exposed England’s shaky performance, leaving them in a “Deep funk.”
