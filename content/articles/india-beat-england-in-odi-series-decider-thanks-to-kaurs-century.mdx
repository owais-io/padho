India finished their England tour with a 13‑run win in the final ODI, clinching a 2‑1 series victory thanks to Harmanpreet Kaur’s 100‑plus score. England almost chased 319 but fell short, with wickets falling in the final overs. Kaur’s century lifted India’s total, while England’s captain struggled with DRS calls and a shaky middle order. The win gives India confidence ahead of the World Cup.
