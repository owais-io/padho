UK Prime Minister Keir Starmer said Britain will let the US use two British military bases to strike Iranian missile sites, saying Iranian attacks are getting more dangerous and could threaten British citizens abroad. The decision came after a suspected drone hit the UK’s Akrotiri base in Cyprus. Many opposition MPs and groups question whether this is legal and risk pulling Britain deeper into a Middle East conflict. The UK says it is defending allies.
