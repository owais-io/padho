In June, the United States and Israel carried out missile strikes on Iranian targets, a move that caught many allies by surprise. European leaders were not consulted, and Gulf countries were caught between public protests and private support for the attack. Russia condemned the strikes and called for a peaceful solution. The operation highlights the shifting alliances and the Trump administration’s willingness to act independently in the Middle East.
