The US and Israel started a big joint bombing campaign against Iran, targeting places like the supreme leader’s home and missile sites. The attacks made people flee the cities. In response, Iran fired missiles at Israel and hit US bases in Gulf countries. The fighting quickly spread to other Middle East nations, with Israel intercepting many missiles. The war caused many casualties and shocked people across the region.
