The Afghan Taliban attacked Pakistani army posts at the border on Saturday night, sparking a fierce gun battle that killed at least 23 Pakistani soldiers and wounded many more. Pakistan retaliated with air strikes and ground raids, claiming it killed about 200 Taliban fighters. The fighting marks a sharp rise in tension between the two countries, which blame each other for supporting terrorist groups that attack the other’s territory.
