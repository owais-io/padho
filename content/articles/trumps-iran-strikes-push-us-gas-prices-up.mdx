Trump’s attacks on Iran have sent US oil prices past $100 a barrel, raising gasoline costs and sparking worries about inflation. Experts say the war shows how fragile our dependence on fossil fuels is, and that a focus on drilling instead of renewables hurts Americans. The strikes even hit oil depots in Tehran, causing fires and toxic smoke. Critics argue that boosting clean energy would give price stability and keep Americans safe.
