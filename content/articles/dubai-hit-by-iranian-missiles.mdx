During a busy weekend in Dubai, tourists and locals were shocked when Iranian drones and missiles struck the city, setting fire to a hotel, airports, and the famous Burj Al Arab. Many people thought the lights were fireworks. The attack caused one death and many injuries, closed airports for days, and shook Dubai’s reputation for safety. Authorities said most missiles were intercepted and urged calm.
