Indian Prime Minister Narendra Modi visited China after seven years. He met with President Xi Jinping and Russian President Vladimir Putin, showing a friendly side that surprised many. The meeting came after U.S. President Donald Trump slapped India with high tariffs. Modi and Xi said they want to calm border disputes and boost trade. The talks help India because it needs Chinese supplies, but the long‑standing mistrust over the Himalayas and Pakistan remains a big problem.
