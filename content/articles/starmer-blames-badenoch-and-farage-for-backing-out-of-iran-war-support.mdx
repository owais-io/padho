During a heated session of Prime Minister’s Questions, Keir Starmer slammed opposition leaders Kemi Badenoch and Nigel Farage for changing their stance on the UK’s involvement in the Iran conflict. He accused them of making “the mother of all U‑turns” and said they had misled the public. Starmer also criticised their remarks about the armed forces and warned that the war could raise fuel and energy costs for ordinary people.
