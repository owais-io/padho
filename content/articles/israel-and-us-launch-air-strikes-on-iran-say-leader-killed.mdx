Israel and the U.S. launched air attacks on Iran, aiming to topple its leadership. Israeli prime minister Netanyahu said the strikes had killed Iran's supreme leader, Ayatollah Khamenei, though no confirmation yet. Iran retaliated with strikes on U.S. bases and Israel, causing casualties and explosions across the region. The conflict threatens to spread, drawing in other Middle Eastern countries and raising fears of wider war and market shocks. World leaders urged de‑escalation, while U.S. and Israeli forces prepare for continued operations.
