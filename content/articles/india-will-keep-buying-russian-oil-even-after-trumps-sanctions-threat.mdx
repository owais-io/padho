India says it will keep buying Russian oil even after Trump threatened sanctions. U.S. announced 25% tariffs on Indian goods and penalties for buying Russian energy. Indian officials deny any halt, saying purchases depend on price and contracts, not politics. Russia supplies about 35% of India’s oil. Long‑term deals make sudden stops difficult. India follows international rules and keeps buying discounted Russian crude to keep fuel prices low.
