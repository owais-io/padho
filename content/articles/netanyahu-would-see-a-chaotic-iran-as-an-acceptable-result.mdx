Netanyahu opposed any land concessions, claiming empty land would become Iranian‑backed launchpads. Iran’s near‑nuclear weapons and proxy network keep the threat alive. After Hamas’s 2023 attack, Israel’s war expanded, fueling plans for regime change and more land grabs. The author urges Israel to pursue peace with Palestinians and neighbours.
