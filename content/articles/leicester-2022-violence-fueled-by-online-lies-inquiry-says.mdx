An independent report says the May‑September 2022 clashes in Leicester between Hindu and Muslim youths were sparked by online misinformation and a failure of local leaders to step in. The city’s mayor, council and police were found to have ignored calls for help, while police lacked good intelligence. The study calls for a permanent community forum and better training for officers on religious tensions.
