The article says the U.S. and Israel want to topple Iran’s dictator and stop its attacks on neighbours, but the plan is shaky. Trump’s messages keep changing, and history shows that bombing alone rarely removes a regime – Iraq and Libya are examples. Iran’s power stays strong, its oil chokehold hurts the world, and Russia gains. Netanyahu and Trump keep pushing bombs without clear goals, risking more bloodshed and possibly making Iran build a nuclear bomb.
