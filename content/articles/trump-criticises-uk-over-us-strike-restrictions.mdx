Donald Trump has publicly slammed UK Prime Minister Keir Starmer for refusing to let the US carry out early attacks on Iran from British air bases. Trump said the UK is not cooperating and even threatened to cut trade with Spain over a similar refusal. Starmer said the UK will allow the US to use Diego Garcia for defensive strikes but wants to keep British interests first. Most Britons oppose US strikes on Iran.
