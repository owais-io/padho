England were thumped by India in a T20 match at Trent Bridge. They were dismissed for 113, their biggest defeat in runs. India chased with ease, scoring 210/5, thanks to a 100 from Smriti Mandhana. England's batting collapsed, and their captain missed the game after a head injury. India’s bowlers, especially Shree Charani, put England under pressure from the start.
