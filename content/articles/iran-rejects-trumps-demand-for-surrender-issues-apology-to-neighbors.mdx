President Masoud Pezeshkian said Iran would never give up, rejecting Donald Trump’s demand for unconditional surrender. He apologized to Gulf neighbors for attacks that hit civilian targets, even though Iran keeps launching drones and missiles. The war began after Israel and the US struck Iran’s top leader, and has spread to Lebanon, Saudi Arabia and the UAE. It has cut oil supplies, slowed air travel and killed thousands of civilians.
