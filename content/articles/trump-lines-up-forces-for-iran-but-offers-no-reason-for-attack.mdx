President Trump has positioned a military force near Iran, yet he has never clearly explained why a war would be necessary. While the public largely opposes new conflicts, Trump withdrew the U.S. from the 2015 nuclear deal and has repeatedly warned that Iran is building weapons. He claims the country’s nuclear sites were destroyed, but U.N. inspectors say many remain functional. With no clear plan or congressional debate, the move raises doubts about whether the U.S. truly faces an imminent threat.
