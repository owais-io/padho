India’s Supreme Court has ordered a probe into Vantara, a huge private zoo run by Mukesh Ambani’s son, after reports of illegal wildlife imports and possible money‑laundering. The park, home to thousands of animals, is closed to the public and has faced backlash from activists. Judges will investigate the acquisition of animals, especially elephants, and check if the harsh climate suits them. The zoo says it will cooperate fully.
