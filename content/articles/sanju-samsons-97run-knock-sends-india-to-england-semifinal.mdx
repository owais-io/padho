Sanju Samson hit an unbeaten 97 for India, sending them past West Indies to reach the semi‑finals of the T20 World Cup. England will face India in Mumbai, while New Zealand and South Africa meet in the other semi‑final. The tournament has had a confusing schedule, with venues changing last‑minute and tickets sold weeks early. The final will still be played in India.
