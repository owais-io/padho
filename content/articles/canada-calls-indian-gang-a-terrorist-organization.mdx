Canada has officially labeled the Lawrence Bishnoi gang a terrorist group after accusations that the gang helped kill Sikh activist Hardeep Singh Nijjar in Vancouver. The move lets Canadian authorities seize the gang’s assets and gives law‑enforcement stronger tools to stop its violent acts. Police say the gang is linked to the Indian government and has been involved in high‑profile murders, extortion, and attacks on Canadian Sikh communities. This designation comes amid a tense Canada‑India diplomatic dispute.
