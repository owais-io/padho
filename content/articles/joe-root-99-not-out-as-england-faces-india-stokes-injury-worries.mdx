In the third Test, England struggled on a slow, low‑scoring pitch. Joe Root steadied the innings, finishing the day unbeaten on 99 runs and pushing England to 251 for 4. Ben Stokes, who had a groin problem, got off to a promising start with 39 not out, but his injury raised concerns for the rest of the match. India’s fast bowlers, especially Jasprit Bumrah, made the pitch tough and kept England’s run‑scoring hard.
