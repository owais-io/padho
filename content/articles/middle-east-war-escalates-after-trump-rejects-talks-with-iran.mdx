Israel and the U.S. carried out several airstrikes on Iran, hitting command centers and missile sites. Iran fired back with missiles and drones at Israel, Saudi Arabia and other Gulf states, including U.S. bases. President Trump said he would not negotiate with Tehran and warned the U.S. might keep fighting for weeks. The fighting has spread to Lebanon, where Israel moved troops and Hezbollah fired missiles. Hundreds have died, especially in Iran, and oil prices are rising.
