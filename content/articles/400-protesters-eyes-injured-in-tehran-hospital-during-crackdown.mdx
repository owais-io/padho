A Tehran eye doctor says more than 400 protester eyes were wounded by gunshots at one hospital during Iran’s harsh crackdown on demonstrations. Doctors report many wounded faces and heads, especially the eyes, and hospitals are overloaded. The attacks come after 2022 protests over women's rights, and authorities are being accused of targeting protester eyes. The story highlights the severe impact on medical staff and the wounded.
