President Masoud Pezeshkian told crowds on the 1979 revolution anniversary that Iran has no plans to build a nuclear weapon. He said the government felt shame over the violent crackdown on protests and promised to listen to the people. At the same time, talks with the U.S. are stalled, with America threatening military action. Iran also keeps its missile program out of the talks, while reformists face arrests.
