Police in Tamil Nadu have charged three leaders of actor‑turned‑politician Vijay’s party after a crowd crush at his rally killed 40 and injured 124. The event drew about 27,000 people, double the 10,000 limit, and took place on a hot day with little food or water. Vijay was not charged, but he stopped speaking when chaos began. The state has set up a commission to investigate the tragedy.
