India is demanding that Australian authorities punish the thieves who stole a bronze Gandhi statue from the Australian Indian Community Centre in Melbourne’s Rowville. Police think three men used an angle grinder to cut it out, and the Indian government has already lodged the complaint with Australian officials. Victoria’s leaders have offered support and warned scrap dealers not to buy the missing statue. The theft follows earlier vandalism incidents of the statue.
