UK Prime Minister Keir Starmer says the conflict with Iran will last longer and the best way forward is to talk, not to fire. He rejected U.S. and Israel’s planned strikes and said Britain will keep its troops and aircraft ready to protect its people in Cyprus. Starmer wants Iran to drop its nuclear ambitions and wants the U.S. to calm the situation. Most Brits in a poll back a defensive approach.
