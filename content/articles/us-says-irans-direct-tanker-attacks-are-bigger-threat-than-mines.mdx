US intelligence says that Iran’s most dangerous threat to ships passing through the Strait of Hormuz is a direct hit from drones or missiles, not mines. Even though the US struck 16 Iranian mine‑laying vessels, one missile or drone could sink a tanker. The strait is closed by Iran, raising oil prices, and lawmakers are worried the US can’t guarantee safe passage.
