Doctors from across Iran formed a network to track injuries after protests. They saw many dead people who were shot or stabbed, and found bodies being moved in ice‑cream trucks and buried in mass graves. The government says about 3,000 died, but doctors estimate more than 30,000. Hospitals were overwhelmed, and many corpses disappeared from forensic records. The doctors fear the true death toll will stay hidden.
