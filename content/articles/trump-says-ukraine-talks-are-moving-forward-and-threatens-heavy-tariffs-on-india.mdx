Trump says the U.S. made good progress in talks with Russia about ending the Ukraine war, and he may meet President Putin next week to keep the conversation going. At the same time, Trump is threatening a 50% tariff on Indian goods because India is buying Russian oil. He also praised Apple’s $100 billion pledge to build more in the U.S. and announced a 100% tariff on foreign computer chips, but spared U.S. chipmakers.
