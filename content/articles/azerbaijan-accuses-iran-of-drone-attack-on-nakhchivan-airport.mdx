Azerbaijan says Iran launched a drone attack that hit its Nakhchivan airport, killing or injuring four people. The Azerbaijani president demanded an apology and said the country will prepare a response. Iran denies responsibility. The incident could widen tensions in the region and risk disrupting air routes that avoid Russian airspace. The attack is the first known Iranian strike on a Caucasus state since the war began.
