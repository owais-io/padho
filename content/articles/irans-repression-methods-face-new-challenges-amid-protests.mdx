Iran's government has blocked the internet to stop protests from spreading, but this shows how uncertain the situation is. People are upset about high inflation and a weak currency. Past movements were harshly crushed, and now the current protests, while smaller, are spreading fast and calling for the regime's fall. The Supreme Leader is hardening his crackdown, and foreign pressure from the US and Israel adds to the chaos. The regime's future is unclear, and the country faces more danger.
