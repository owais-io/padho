US and Israel are bombing Iran, hoping to break the regime. Early strikes hit key leaders, but Iran still has weapons and plans to widen the fight. If the war keeps growing, it could spread to the Gulf, hit oil shipping, and cost Washington more money and time. Experts warn that military success might not bring political goals, pushing sides into deeper conflict. The situation could spiral out of hand and affect the whole region.
