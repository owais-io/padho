Kevin Pietersen defended England captain Ben Stokes after critics in India and Australia slammed the team's reaction in the fourth Test at Old Trafford. Stokes was blamed for letting the match slip when Indian players stayed on to reach centuries, causing England to ease off the field. Pietersen said those watching from home shouldn’t judge players in the heat of the game. He posted this on X. England lead 2‑1 and the final Test is set at Oval.
