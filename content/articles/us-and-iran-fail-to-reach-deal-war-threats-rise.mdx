Talks between the U.S. and Iran about Iran’s nuclear program ended without an agreement. The U.S. is ready to use a large military force in the Middle East if talks fail. Iran insists it can keep low‑level uranium enrichment under U.N. checks, while the U.S. wants a complete halt. Both sides say they will meet again soon, but the talks ended with no progress on key issues.
