Trump says he’s ready to use military force against Iran, White House confirms. Although diplomacy is first choice, the president might use airstrikes if necessary. Iran is in turmoil, with protests over a weak rial and a crackdown that killed hundreds. Trump announced a 25% tariff on trade with Iran and threatened retaliation if the regime uses violence. The White House stresses the president wants to avoid killing civilians but will act if it must.
