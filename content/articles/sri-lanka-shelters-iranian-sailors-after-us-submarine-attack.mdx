Sri Lanka helped 208 Iranian sailors after a US submarine sank an Iranian warship the day before. The sailors were brought to Sri Lanka's port at Trincomalee and then taken to a naval base near Colombo. Sri Lanka said it was staying neutral and only acting to save lives. The US attack was part of a larger conflict between the US and Iran, which has caused fear in local hospitals and the community.
