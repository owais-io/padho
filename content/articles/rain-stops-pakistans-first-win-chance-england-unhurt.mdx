Pakistan were almost ready to beat England for their first one‑day international win. They were 34 for no wickets chasing 113 when heavy rain interrupted the game. England had already struggled in this World Cup and were 79 for seven at the time of the delay. The match was called off, giving England a no‑result and keeping their chance of reaching the semi‑finals alive.
