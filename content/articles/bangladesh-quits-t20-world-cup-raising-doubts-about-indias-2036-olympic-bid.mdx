Bangladesh pulled out of the next T20 World Cup after the ICC refused to move their matches from India to Sri Lanka. The decision was linked to a row over a Bangladeshi bowler being dropped from an IPL team and the tense ties between Bangladesh and India. The incident worries the International Olympic Committee, because India is bidding to host the 2036 Olympics and cricket could be on the Games’ programme. If other countries boycotted, India’s Olympic chances could suffer.
