The Guardian article shares stories of ordinary Iranians struggling with economic hardship, job loss and uncertainty while protests continue. A fashion designer, a clothing entrepreneur, a sports coach, a gold trader and others explain how the fall of the Islamic Republic could bring hope but also fear of chaos and long recovery times. Most feel protests must be sustained, that change will be slow, and their personal future is bleak without a clear plan.
