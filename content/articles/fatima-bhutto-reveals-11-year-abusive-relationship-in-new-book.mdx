Fatima Bhutto, a famous Pakistani writer, says she spent 11 years in an abusive, controlling relationship. She wrote about it in her new book, The Hour of the Wolf, after struggling to focus on her dog and her career during the pandemic. The book reveals how her partner isolated her, used violence, and made her believe his harsh moments were love. Now she’s married, has two children, and keeps her family safe.
