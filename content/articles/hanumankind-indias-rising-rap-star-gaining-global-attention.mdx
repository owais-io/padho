Hanumankind, real name Sooraj Cherukat from Kerala, is a 32‑year‑old rapper who’s shot up in popularity with hits like “Big Dawgs” and “Run It Up.” After a leg injury during a UK gig, he’s still touring, releasing a new mixtape called *Monsoon Season*, and working with big names such as A$AP Rocky. He mixes English lyrics with Indian drums and hopes to use his fame to talk about social issues and break stereotypes.
