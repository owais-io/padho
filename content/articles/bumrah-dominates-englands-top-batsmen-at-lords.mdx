Bumrah took five wickets for 74 runs in his first spell at Lord’s, crushing England’s top batsmen like Joe Root and Ben Stokes. His quick, clean bowling and surprising accuracy made India look strong. The fast‑bowler has an amazing record, with 215 wickets at an average under 19.5, the best of any bowler with over 200 wickets. Fans saw him use his unique run‑up and powerful arm to dominate the match.
