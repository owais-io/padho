Russia is reviving the old Soviet song contest Intervision, set to start Saturday in Moscow. About 23 nations, including China, India, Brazil, South Africa, Belarus and Central Asian states, will compete. The show will focus on patriotic ballads and “traditional values” instead of pop glitter. Russia’s entry is pro‑war singer Shaman. Presenters are Chinese star Lay and Indian actor Stefy Patel. The U.S. singer Brandon Howard quit. The event follows Russia’s 2022 Eurovision ban after the Ukraine war.
