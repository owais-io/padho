Lawmakers criticized President Trump for launching airstrikes on Iran without notifying Congress, arguing it violates the 1973 War Powers Act. The president said the attacks were urgent but gave no 48‑hour warning. Senators and representatives called the strikes illegal, urged a congressional review, and warned of a new conflict. Some Republicans defended the move, while Democrats and media commentators condemned it as an abuse of presidential power.
