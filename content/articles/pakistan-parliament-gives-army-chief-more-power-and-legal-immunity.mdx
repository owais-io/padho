Pakistan's parliament just passed a controversial amendment that boosts the army chief's power and gives him lifelong immunity from prosecution. Field Marshal Asim Munir will lead all branches of the military and will be protected from any criminal case. The change also weakens the Supreme Court, creating a new court controlled by the president. Critics say this turns Pakistan toward authoritarian rule.
