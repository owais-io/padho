Iran’s women’s football squad is heading to Australia for the 2026 Asian Cup, but they are under huge stress. Recent protests and harsh police crackdowns in Iran have left players fearing for their safety, family pressure, and possible punishment. Some team members have already quit, and contact with the squad is almost impossible because of an internet shutdown. The team’s future now depends on how the Iranian government deals with these players.
