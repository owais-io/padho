Trump’s press‑conference answers about the Iran attack have been mixed. He says the war is over, then that it has just begun. The President avoids the usual calm speeches, instead tweeting or talking off‑the‑cuff. This confusion makes it hard for the public and allies to know the US goal, hurts trust, and leaves the nation split on whether the move was right. Congress may not back him.
