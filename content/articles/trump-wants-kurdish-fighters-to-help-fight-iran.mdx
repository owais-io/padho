Trump wants Kurdish fighters to go to Iran’s borders, hoping they’ll take small areas and force Iran to spread its troops. The Kurds, 30‑40 million people with no state, live in Turkey, Iraq, Iran, Syria and Armenia and are known for their tough militia, the peshmerga. The U.S. helped them defeat ISIS, but Kurdish leaders say they will stay neutral, warning that the plan could drag them into a new war.
