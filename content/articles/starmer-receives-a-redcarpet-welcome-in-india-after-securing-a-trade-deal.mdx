Prime Minister Keir Starmer visited India and was greeted with huge posters and a red carpet. The trip celebrated a new trade agreement that he said earlier leaders missed. He met India’s PM Modi, who gave him a gingerbread souvenir, and talked to UK business leaders about the deal and the upcoming UK budget. The trade deal will add only a tiny boost to the UK economy.
