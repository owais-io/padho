The first report on Air India Flight 171, which crashed on 12 June killing 260, shows that the plane’s fuel switches were pulled to “cutoff” moments after it left the ground. The switch mechanism is hard to move accidentally, and the pilots said nothing about it. The aircraft lost engine power, tried to restart, and then crashed. No weather, bird, or bad fuel issues were found. Investigation continues.
