Pakistan cricket has gone through a lot of changes. The new Test captain, Shaheen Afridi, has taken the team but results have been poor. The board and coaching staff have changed often, and the team is not playing in the Indian Premier League, missing a chance to learn from the best. A Test series against England next summer could be a turning point, giving players a chance to prove themselves and bring hope back.
