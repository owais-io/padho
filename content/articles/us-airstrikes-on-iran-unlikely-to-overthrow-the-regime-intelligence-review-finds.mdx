The U.S. and Israel have been bombing Iran hoping to topple its leaders, but an intelligence review says the attacks are unlikely to overthrow the Iranian regime. Democrats fear that the airstrikes are draining U.S. weapon supplies needed by allies like Ukraine. The campaign has caused civilian casualties, including a school strike that killed dozens. Trump praised the strikes, while Lockheed Martin is boosting munitions production.
