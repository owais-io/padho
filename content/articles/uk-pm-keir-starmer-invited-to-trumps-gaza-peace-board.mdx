The Guardian reports that British Prime Minister Keir Starmer has been asked to join a peace board set up by former U.S. President Donald Trump to help stop fighting and rebuild Gaza. Starmer is said to be likely to accept, but no formal invitation has been sent yet. Trump plans to chair the board, with other world leaders on it. The group would oversee Gaza’s temporary peace and reconstruction, but details are still being worked out.
