Trump said he’s not satisfied with Iran’s nuclear talks and that a military strike could still happen. He keeps diplomacy on the table but says Iran must never get a nuclear weapon. While Oman’s diplomat says talks are making progress, U.S. officials warn Americans to leave Israel and the U.S. is ready to move two aircraft carriers into the region. Iran says it will keep enriching only for peaceful use and wants UN inspectors.
