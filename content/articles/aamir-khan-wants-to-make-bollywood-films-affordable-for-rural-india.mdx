Aamir Khan, a top Bollywood star, said that most Indians never go to the cinema because tickets are too expensive and many rural areas lack theatres. He plans to release his movies on YouTube for just 100 rupees (about $1) so families can watch at home. Khan hopes this will bring films back to the 97% of people who can’t afford a multiplex, while keeping the big screen important.
