At a press event, President Trump said he’s worried that if Iran’s leader is removed, a new ruler could be just as bad as the old one. He praised the US‑Israeli military push against Iran, but warned the change might bring another harsh regime. Trump also criticized Spain for not allowing US bases and the UK for slow cooperation, calling both unhelpful. The comments came while German chancellor Friedrich Merz visited the White House.
