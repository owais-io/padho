A former high‑school student from Canberra may have been executed in Iran after being detained, raising concerns in Australia. The government has asked Tehran to stop executions and offered support to classmates. The case highlights Iran’s use of the death penalty amid protests that have killed thousands. Australia says it opposes capital punishment in all circumstances and is pressuring Iran to end it.
