Israel attacked Hezbollah positions in Beirut and southern Lebanon after the group fired missiles at Israel in response to the death of Iran’s top leader. At least 31 people died and 149 were hurt, and the army asked people in 50 villages to leave. Roads were jammed as civilians fled. U.S. and Israel kept striking Iranian targets, raising oil prices and canceling flights. Trump warned of more strikes, while a UK base in Cyprus was hit by a suspected drone.
