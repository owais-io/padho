On Wednesday, Iran stepped up attacks on Gulf shipping and infrastructure, striking commercial ships and Dubai’s airport while U.S. and Israeli planes launched new strikes. The fighting, part of a 12‑day war, has also seen Israeli raids on Hezbollah targets and Iranian missiles hit Israel. The clashes have hurt many civilians, sent thousands of people fleeing, and pushed oil prices higher. The U.N. demanded a stop, and the U.S. released barrels of oil to calm markets.
