England barely beat Nepal by four runs in a tense T20 World Cup match in Mumbai. Sam Curran, who bowled the final over, said the narrow victory is a big boost for the squad. He praised the improvement of associate teams and stressed that close wins give the team a confidence boost. England will face the West Indies next. They plan to use this experience to prepare for future tight games.
