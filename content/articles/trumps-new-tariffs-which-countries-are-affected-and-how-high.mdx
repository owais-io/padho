Trump’s administration has slapped new tariffs on imports from dozens of countries, starting at 10% and climbing to 41%. The measures went live just after midnight Thursday. Brazil faces a 10% base rate plus a 40% extra penalty over former President Bolsonaro, totaling 50%. The White House warns India could face a 25% hike, also bringing it to 50%, but India has 21 days to protest. Other unnamed countries get a 10% rate.
