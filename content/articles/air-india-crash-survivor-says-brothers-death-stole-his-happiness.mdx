Vishwash Kumar Ramesh, the only person who survived the Air India flight that crashed near Ahmedabad in June, says he is lucky to be alive but feels his life is empty because his brother died on the same plane. He has flashbacks, sleeps only a few hours, and his family is deeply affected. The crash killed 241 people and left Ramesh and others with deep trauma.
