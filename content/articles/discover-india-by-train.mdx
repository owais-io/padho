Indian trains let you experience the country’s variety in a single ride. From crowded sleeper compartments to roadside stalls selling fresh fruit and local snacks, you meet people from all walks of life. The author shares stories of long delays, food, and surprising encounters. Despite delays and occasional dangers, train journeys are affordable, eco‑friendly, and a window into India’s daily life.
