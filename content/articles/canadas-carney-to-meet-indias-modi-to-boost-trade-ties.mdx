Canadian Finance Minister Mark Carney will travel to India to meet Prime Minister Narendra Modi. The two leaders want to boost trade and fix a long‑standing diplomatic rift. Canada is looking to sell more goods outside the US after a tough relationship with Washington. India, the world’s biggest importer of energy, could buy Canadian uranium and other supplies. The visit comes amid security worries over past Canadian protests and India’s actions.
