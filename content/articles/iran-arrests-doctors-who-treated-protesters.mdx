In Iran, medical workers who treated injured protestors are being arrested and even face the death penalty. Doctors, including surgeon Alireza Golchini, have been detained for helping wounded people, with authorities claiming they “waged war against God.” Human rights groups say the arrests are part of a revenge campaign. The U.S. and other rights groups call for their release, warning the regime will punish anyone who treats protest injuries.
