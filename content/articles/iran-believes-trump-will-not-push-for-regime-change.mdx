In early 2024, Iran says Trump isn’t planning an attack or a government shift. After Israel’s June strike and U.S. missile hits on nuclear sites, Iran’s economy collapsed and protests erupted. Tehran still wants hard‑talk, claiming it can survive any conflict. U.S. officials admit they have no clear plan for Iran’s future. Some Iranians welcome foreign pressure, others fear it would hurt their chance for democracy.
