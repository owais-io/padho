The UK defence secretary says Russian support is shaping Iran’s use of drones against western bases. After drones hit a UK base in Iraq, officials said Iran’s pilots are copying Russian tactics, flying low for more hits. Russia is believed to have shared how to use Shahed drones, which Iran has fired over 2,000 times. The attacks threaten the Strait of Hormuz, raising oil prices. Britain says more cooperation may be needed to protect shipping.
