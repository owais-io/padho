England’s new coach Charlotte Edwards is focused on improving the team before the World Cup. In the first match of a three‑game series, India won by four wickets, showing solid batting and bowling. England struggled after early wickets, but Sophia Dunkley and Alice Davidson‑Richards steadied the innings with a 106‑run partnership. India chased the target confidently, ending with 10 balls left. The game highlighted England’s need for stronger middle‑order batting.
