Iran has effectively shut the Strait of Hormuz, the main oil shipping lane, by striking at least four tankers with drones. The attack has cut sea traffic by about 80 %, and insurers have pulled coverage. While U.S. forces hit Iran’s small navy, Iran warns it will burn any ship that tries to cross. The move has pushed global oil prices higher and may lead the U.S. to help tankers get new insurance.
