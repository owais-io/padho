On Saturday, U.S. and Israel carried out a daylight bombing campaign in Tehran, targeting the Iranian leadership and government buildings. The strike aimed to kill top officials and force a regime change. The attacks caused civilian casualties, and the Iranian government responded with missile strikes across the Gulf, threatening U.S. bases and Gulf states. The move sparked diplomatic backlash, with some urging a pause on negotiations and raising fears of a wider war.
