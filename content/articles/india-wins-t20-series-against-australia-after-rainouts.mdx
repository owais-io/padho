India clinched the T20 series 2‑1 after two matches were cancelled by rain. Captain Suryakumar Yadav celebrated the win, thanking his team for bouncing back after a first‑match loss. Abhishek Sharma was named player of the series for his steady batting. The final game at Brisbane’s Gabba was also washed out, leaving India as the series champions. Weather kept fans on their toes, but the series still finished in India’s favour.
