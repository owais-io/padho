John Healey, Britain’s defence secretary, said that Iran’s drone attacks are getting better because of help from Russia. He pointed out a recent drone strike on a British‑led base in Iraq, and argued that the two countries share tactics. Healey’s comments tie the Ukraine war to the Middle East, and raise questions about Britain’s role in future conflicts and its relationship with the US.
