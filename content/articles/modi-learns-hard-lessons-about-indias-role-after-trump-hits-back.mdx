Trump slapped India with a 50% tariff after the country bought Russian oil, hurting exports and breaking the promise of a close US partnership that Modi had hoped for. Modi’s friendly rapport with Trump crumbled as the US also downplayed India’s role and treated India and Pakistan as petty rivals. The move shows India must balance its independent stance with a heavy reliance on the US, while navigating a tougher world order.
