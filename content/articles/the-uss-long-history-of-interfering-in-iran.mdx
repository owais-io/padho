The piece argues that the United States has a long record of meddling in Iran. It cites the 1953 coup that removed elected Prime Mossadegh, support for the Shah’s brutal regime, arming Saddam Hussein, and backing Israel in attacks on Iranian civilians. The writer says Trump’s focus on Iran distracts from American domestic repression. It also stresses that Iranians want a democratic republic, not a return to the monarchy, and urges the world to hear their voice.
