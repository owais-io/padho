The US and Israel carried out a strike on Iran that has killed more than 1,000 civilians, many children, according to a U.S. rights group. Iran is retaliating by striking U.S. bases and civilian sites across the region, even in Oman, and is threatening to widen the conflict. Gulf leaders are angry but cautious about taking action. The fighting is driving up energy prices and unsettling markets worldwide.
