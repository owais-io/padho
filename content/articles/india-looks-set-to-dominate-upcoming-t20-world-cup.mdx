The next T20 World Cup will take place in 2026, and the game is moving faster than ever. Runs are coming at a record pace, with many matches seeing teams hit 200+ quickly. India will play all the matches at home, giving them a huge edge – they have won 31 of 41 games overall and 14 of 17 at home. England and Australia are the main challengers, but India’s talent and home comfort make them the clear favorites.
