Intense airstrikes hit military sites on Iran’s northern border with Iraq as the U.S. and Israel prepare to support Kurdish forces if they cross the line. A U.S. official said air help is ready for Kurdish fighters. Meanwhile, Baloch militants have moved from Pakistan into Iran, raising fears of a civil war if Tehran falls. U.S. officials said they may not be able to shoot down every Iranian drone but will target launch sites.
