Trump said the US has an “armada” heading to Middle East as Iran’s death toll from a crackdown on protests tops 5,000. He said the US is watching Iran closely and may not need to use force. The protests began in late December over falling rial and have turned into the deadliest unrest since 1979. UN officials condemned violence and called for an end to repression. The US and UK are preparing military assets, but both sides are urging diplomacy.
