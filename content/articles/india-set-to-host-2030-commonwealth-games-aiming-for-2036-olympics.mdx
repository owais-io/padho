India’s Ahmedabad will host the Commonwealth Games in 2030, after Commonwealth Sport’s board chose it over Nigeria. This win is part of India’s push to stage the 2036 Olympics. The IOC still worries about past mismanagement and doping, but the 2030 Games could improve India’s reputation and prove it can run a major event. Other hopeful cities like Qatar and Istanbul still eye future Olympics, but India’s chance looks strong.
