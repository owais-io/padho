Ali Khamenei, Iran’s top leader, admitted that thousands of people died during the two‑week protests, calling some killings “inhuman” and blaming the United States. He urged harsh punishment for the demonstrators. Human‑rights groups say over 3,000 people have been killed and 22,000 arrested. The protests started over a drop in the currency, grew into a demand to overthrow the government, and led to a heavy‑handed crackdown and a week‑long internet blackout.
