After an Israeli strike killed Iran’s Supreme Leader, the US and Israel began a new round of attacks on Iranian targets. The strikes hit Tehran’s heart, aimed at destroying air defenses and encouraging a government change. Iran said it would retaliate and warned against foreign interference. The fighting has sparked fears of wider war, disrupted flights in Dubai and Doha, and raised oil‑price worries. Some Iranians hope the strikes could spark a revolt, but many fear more civilian loss.
