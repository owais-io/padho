A US military probe says a Tomahawk missile that hit an elementary school in Minab, Iran, was a targeting error. The strike on Feb. 28 killed about 175 people, most of them children. Video and satellite evidence show the missile was American. President Trump denied U.S. responsibility, but the Pentagon said the incident is still being investigated. The findings confirm Iran’s claim that the U.S. caused the tragedy.
