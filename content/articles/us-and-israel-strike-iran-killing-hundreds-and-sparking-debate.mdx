U.S. and Israeli forces carried out airstrikes on Iran last Saturday, killing hundreds of Iranian civilians and sparking fears of a larger conflict. The attacks also claimed six U.S. service members. Critics say the moves go against former President Donald Trump’s long‑standing pledge to pull back from foreign wars and risk dragging the U.S. into a drawn‑out Middle East showdown. Supporters argue the strikes are needed to deter Iran and protect U.S. allies.
