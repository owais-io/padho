Heavy rains this week hit Hong Kong, India and Japan, causing floods, landslides and major travel disruptions. Hong Kong received four black‑rain warnings, while Uttarakhand in India saw over 200 mm in a day, killing one person and leaving 50 missing. Kanazawa, Japan’s west coast, got 148 mm in three hours, shutting roads and bullet trains. Weather models say more rain will hit Japan, while Europe is preparing for a heatwave with temperatures over 40 °C.
