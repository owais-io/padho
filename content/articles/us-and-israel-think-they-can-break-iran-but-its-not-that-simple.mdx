US and Israel have launched a series of air strikes on Iran, aiming to destroy its defenses, shut down missile factories, and kill top leaders. They think this will collapse the regime. In reality, Iran has survived, using small drones and missiles to stretch the fight and stay alive. The attacks don’t guarantee an uprising, and the war could spill over to the region.
