To avoid a possible U.S. strike, Iran’s foreign minister Abbas Araghchi will travel to Ankara for talks. Turkey wants Tehran to give up its nuclear program in exchange for peace. The U.S. has warned that any attack would be huge, while Iran says it can fight back with drones and missiles. Inside Iran, protests and a split between supporters of the regime and those demanding change add to the tension.
