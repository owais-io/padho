An FBI alert about a possible Iranian drone attack on California caused panic after ABC News reported it. State leaders, the FBI, and the White House said the warning was based on unverified information and that there was no real threat. California officials said the state was prepared but not at risk. Police increased patrols around key sites for safety, but the threat was dismissed as unfounded.
