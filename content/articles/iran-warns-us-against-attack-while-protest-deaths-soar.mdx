Protests in Iran have turned deadly, with human‑rights groups saying hundreds have died. The Iranian government is cracking down hard, arresting more than 10,000 people and cutting off the internet. Tehran’s president has warned that the US should not attack, while Donald Trump has said he could step in to help the protesters. The situation could lead to more violence or even foreign intervention.
