Marco Rubio told US ambassadors in the Middle East to stop saying things that could spark anger or hurt talks with Iran about nuclear weapons. The memo was aimed at the US ambassador to Israel, who said Israel had a biblical claim to much of the region. Rubio’s warning came as Trump’s team was trying to get Iran to give up its nuclear sites. The move shows how delicate the talks are.
