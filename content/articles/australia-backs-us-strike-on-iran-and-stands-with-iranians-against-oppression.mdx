Australian Prime Minister Anthony Albanese said his country supports the U.S. strike on Iran aimed at stopping its nuclear program and warns that Iran may retaliate, especially against Australians. He condemned Iran’s regime for destabilising the Middle East, attacking Australian soil and oppressing its own people. Australia has raised travel alerts for Israel, Lebanon and Iran, and advised citizens to leave if safe. Political leaders in Australia have mixed reactions, with some urging restraint.
