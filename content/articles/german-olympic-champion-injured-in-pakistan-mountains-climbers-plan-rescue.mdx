Laura Dahlmeier, a former German biathlon star, was seriously hurt when rocks fell on Laila Peak in Pakistan. A helicopter can’t reach her because of the high altitude and danger of more rockfalls. A team of four climbers—three Americans and a German—will try to get to her on foot. The rescue is risky, but hope is high that she will survive.
