A 47‑year‑old Pakistani businessman, Asif Merchant, is on trial in Brooklyn for a plot to kill former U.S. President Donald Trump. He says Iran’s Revolutionary Guard threatened his family to force him into the scheme. While he testified in his own defense, prosecutors argue there is no evidence he was truly under duress. The case unfolds amid recent U.S. strikes on Iran and heightened tensions over alleged Iranian plots against U.S. officials.
