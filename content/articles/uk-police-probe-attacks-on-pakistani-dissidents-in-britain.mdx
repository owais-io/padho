Two Pakistani critics of former PM Imran Khan living in the UK have been hit with a series of violent attacks. The first hit on Christmas Eve was a punch‑fight, the second a gunshot to a front window, and a third involved a fire‑rag. Scotland Yard’s counter‑terror unit is looking into the crimes and has arrested one man on suspicion of arson and weapons use. The attacks may be linked to foreign state‑backed groups.
