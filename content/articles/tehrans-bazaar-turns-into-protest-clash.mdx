In Tehran, security forces clashed with protesters who had gathered for a sit‑in at the historic Grand Bazaar. Officers sprayed tear gas and forced demonstrators out, while video shows troops entering a hospital in Illam province looking for protesters. At least 35 people have died in Iran’s protests, and over 1,200 have been arrested. The country’s economy is in crisis, with the rial at record lows and prices soaring, fueling the unrest.
