The U.S. Navy said it shot down an Iranian drone that was approaching the Abraham Lincoln aircraft carrier in the Arabian Sea. An F‑35 jet intercepted the Shahed‑139 drone in a defensive move, with no U.S. casualties. Iran didn’t confirm the event. The incident happened as U.S. and Iranian officials were trying to set up nuclear talks, while both sides deal with other tensions in the Gulf.
