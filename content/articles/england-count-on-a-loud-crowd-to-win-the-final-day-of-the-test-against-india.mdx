England are hoping the roar of a full Lord’s stands to give them the edge they need to finish the Test at the top of the third day. After India were all out for 192, the fans’ support helped England take the last four wickets and set a 193‑run target. Ben Stokes’ final‑ball dismissal sealed the lead, and the coach said the buzz around the ground was exactly what the team needed.
