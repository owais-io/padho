Pakistan’s government has decided its cricket team will not play the scheduled match against India in the upcoming T20 World Cup, citing political tensions between the two nations. Bangladesh had earlier pulled out over security concerns, and the ICC’s hybrid format keeps India‑Pakistan games on the calendar. The ICC worries the boycott could hurt large TV deals and fan interest, while Pakistan stresses keeping sports and politics separate.
