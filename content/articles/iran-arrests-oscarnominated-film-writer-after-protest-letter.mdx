Mehdi Mahmoudian, co‑writer of the Oscar‑nominated film “It Was Just an Accident,” was arrested in Tehran after signing a letter blaming Iran’s Supreme Leader, Ali Khamenei, for recent violence. He and two other signatories were detained with no charges announced. Mahmoudian, a journalist and human‑rights activist, helped the film’s director Jafar Panahi after being imprisoned. The arrest comes amid huge protests and a disputed death toll.
