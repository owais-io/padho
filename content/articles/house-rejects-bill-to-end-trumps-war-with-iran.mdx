The House voted 212‑219 against a bill that would have forced the U.S. to pull back from fighting Iran. Republicans backed the vote, while Democrats wanted to stop the conflict. The resolution was tied to President Trump’s decision to launch air and naval attacks after a drone strike, and it also criticized the lack of clear goals for the war. The vote came after a similar Senate rejection. Republicans say Trump had the right to act.
