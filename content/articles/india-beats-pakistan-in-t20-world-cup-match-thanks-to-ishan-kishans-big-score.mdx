India defeated Pakistan by 61 runs in the T20 World Cup. Ishan Kishan scored 77 runs off 40 balls, almost all of India's total. Pakistan fell to 114 runs after early wickets, failing to chase 176. India’s bowlers, especially Hardik Pandya and Jasprit Bumrah, took wickets quickly. The match was a clear victory for India, showcasing their strong batting and bowling lineup.
