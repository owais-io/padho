In Iran, big protests have slowed after a harsh crackdown that left thousands dead and many in prison. The government says it has stopped executions, and US President Donald Trump backed down from threats of military strikes. Meanwhile, the UN and some countries say Iran must end violence and release prisoners, while Russia defends Iran. The protests started over a sharp fall in the currency and rising food prices, and many people still want political change.
