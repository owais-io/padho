Conservative media personalities are fighting over Donald Trump’s decision to confront Iran. Tucker Carlson, Megyn Kelly, Mark Levin and Ben Shapiro have taken personal jabs, calling each other cowards and warm‑ongers. The feud shows deeper cracks in the Trump‑MAGA coalition and is used to attract viewers, but it also signals growing division among right‑wing voices. The clash is driven by disagreements about Israel’s influence and the U.S. role in the Middle East.
