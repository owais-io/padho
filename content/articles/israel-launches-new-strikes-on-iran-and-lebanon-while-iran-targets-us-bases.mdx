Israel launched fresh attacks on Tehran and Beirut, while Iran fired missiles toward Israel and U.S. bases in the Gulf. The strikes hit police stations, the IRGC, and a site where Iran was picking a new supreme leader. In Lebanon, bombings killed six people and displaced tens of thousands. Iran also struck U.S. installations in Saudi Arabia, UAE, Bahrain, and Qatar, marking the first time U.S. bases have been hit this far.
