After President Trump said UK soldiers didn’t fight on the frontlines in Afghanistan, he posted a clarification praising the troops and acknowledging 457 deaths. UK politicians from all sides cheered the move, saying the comments were offensive. Trump didn’t apologize, but his words were seen as a step back. The king had also helped push for the change, and many leaders said the UK remains proud of its forces.
