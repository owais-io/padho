India’s new skipper Shubman Gill is in a tight spot on their England tour. The team has struggled with bad bowling choices and injuries to key bowlers like Bumrah and Siraj, leaving the attack uneven. Gill has been quiet on the field, rarely giving the team a lift or clear direction. Fans worry that his calm style might not inspire the side to turn things around.
