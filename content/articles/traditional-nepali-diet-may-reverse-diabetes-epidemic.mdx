Traditional Nepali foods like lentil and rice are being tested to reverse diabetes. In a city study, 70 patients followed an 850‑calorie diet and 43% went into remission. A community trial of 120 people shows similar success, with half free of diabetes after four months and a 4‑5kg weight loss. The program relies on cheap local meals and volunteer support, aiming to curb western junk‑food‑driven diabetes in Nepal.
