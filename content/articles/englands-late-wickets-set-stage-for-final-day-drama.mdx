England’s bowlers, especially Brydon Carse and Ben Stokes, took crucial wickets in the third Test at Lord's, breaking India’s hope of a simple chase. India were set 193 to win after being dismissed for 192, but wickets fell quickly. Carse claimed several key dismissals, while Stokes’ bowling tightened the pressure. With England leading 2‑1, the final day promised drama as India tried to finish the match.
