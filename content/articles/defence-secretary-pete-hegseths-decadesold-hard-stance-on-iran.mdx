Pete Hegseth, now US defence secretary, has for years spoken in very harsh terms about Iran, calling it an existential threat and “America’s mortal enemy.” He has used books, Fox News, and a 2018 Israeli speech to warn that Iran wants nuclear weapons and to support strong military action. Hegseth also ties Israel to his views, using biblical references to justify backing the state. The Guardian’s review shows his long‑standing rhetoric and how it shapes US‑Israel policy.
