Trump said India’s prime minister promised to stop buying Russian oil, but Indian officials denied any phone call with the US president. India remains one of the biggest purchasers of Russian crude, which has led Washington to impose extra tariffs on its imports. The country says it will not let the US control its energy choices and continues to buy cheap Russian oil. The disagreement has widened tensions between New Delhi and Washington.
