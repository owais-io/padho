City Football Group has pulled out of Mumbai City FC after six years, leaving a gap in Indian football. The Indian Super League (ISL) was supposed to start in September, but a 15‑year commercial deal ended and no new partner was found, so the season was delayed and still hasn't begun. Fans, players and clubs are upset, and the move shows big problems with the league's management, risking future investment.
