In 2025 India built a new world‑class women’s team, highlighted by Harmanpreet Kaur’s World Cup win and many record‑setting performances. Smriti Mandhana scored the most runs ever in a year, while South Africa’s Laura Wolvaardt captained her side to great success. Jemimah Rodrigues, Beth Mooney, and Alana King also shone in crucial moments. England’s Nat Sciver‑Brunt led her team as captain, and Australia’s Ash Gardner and Deepti Sharma broke personal bests, proving the year was full of standout talent.
