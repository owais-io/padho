Exiled Awami League leaders in Kolkata are planning to return to Bangladesh and challenge the new interim government. They accuse former president Sheikh Hasina of crimes and claim the 2024 elections will be unfair. While India lets them stay and meet with Hasina, Bangladesh says it wants her extradited. The leaders face charges of murder, corruption and war crimes, but they say they will win back power. The country is tense over the upcoming vote.
