UK officials say any change in Iran’s government must be peaceful and not involve Britain in US plans. Transport Secretary Heidi Alexander said Britain would not support any US military action against Iran, and called for an end to the violence against protesters. Meanwhile Trump has warned he will back protesters with force. Iran’s crackdown has killed hundreds and detained thousands, sparking widespread protests and an internet blackout.
