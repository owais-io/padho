England beat Sri Lanka in a rain‑affected T20 match at Pallekele. Spin bowler Adil Rashid took three wickets and set up a collapse, while Sam Curran finished with a hat‑trick. England’s Phil Salt and Tom Banton gave a solid start, and the rest of the team chased the target just in time, winning the match and the series on the DLS method.
