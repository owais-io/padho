Xi Jinping hosted the Shanghai Cooperation Organisation (SCO) summit in Tianjin, where he met Russia’s Vladimir Putin, India’s Narendra Modi and Turkey’s Recep Tayyip Erdoğan. The leaders discussed trade, security and the idea of a multipolar world that isn’t dominated by the United States. Modi’s visit followed new US tariffs on India’s imports, while China and Russia remain close amid the Ukraine war, with China offering economic help but staying officially neutral.
