The UK is arranging a flight from Oman to bring Britons out of the Middle East after Iran’s attacks on the region. A government‑chartered plane will leave Muscat soon, focusing on vulnerable citizens. Many British nationals have registered with the Foreign Office to receive updates. Airlines like Virgin Atlantic, British Airways and Etihad are also helping with departures. The move comes as Gulf airspace is largely closed and travel is disrupted.
