A passenger bus in Nepal went off a narrow, steep road and plunged 200 metres, killing 19 people, including 24‑year‑old British hiker Dominic Stewart, and injuring 25. The crash happened overnight as the bus was heading from Pokhara to Kathmandu. Rescue teams struggled with poor equipment and the remote site. Nepal’s police set up a taskforce to investigate. The road is known for dangerous bends and has had other deadly accidents before.
