Ukraine’s president said rising tensions between the United States and Iran might push back a planned meeting in Abu Dhabi where Ukrainian and Russian leaders will try to negotiate a pause in fighting. Zelenskyy said the date or location could change. Russia’s envoy will be in Miami. Both sides have cut attacks and are willing to stop strikes if the other does. European leaders differ on supporting Ukraine and on opening talks with Russia.
