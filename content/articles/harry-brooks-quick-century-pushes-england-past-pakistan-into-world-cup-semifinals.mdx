England beat Pakistan 168–164, thanks to Harry Brook’s 100 off just 50 balls. Brook’s fast run‑rate broke the tie, giving England a comfortable win. Pakistan’s top batsmen struggled, and their bowlers failed to keep up. The victory secures England’s place in the T20 World Cup semi‑finals.
