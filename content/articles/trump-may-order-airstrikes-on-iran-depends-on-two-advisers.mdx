Trump is weighing airstrikes on Iran if the country stalls on a nuclear deal. His decision will be guided by advisers Jared Kushner and Steve Witkoff, who will brief him on Tehran’s latest offer ahead of a Geneva negotiation. The president has heard military options and worries about a hard retaliation. The U.S. has moved a carrier and jets to the region to prepare. Other senior officials, including the VP and defense secretary, are also reviewing risks.
