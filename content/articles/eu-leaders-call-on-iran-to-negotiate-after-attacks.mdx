France, Germany and the UK told Iran it must end the war and let its people decide their future. They condemned Iran’s strikes on U.S. bases and urged the regime to stop its missile and nuclear programmes. The EU said it isn’t part of the attacks and is looking for diplomatic ways to calm the region, even calling for a UN Security Council meeting.
