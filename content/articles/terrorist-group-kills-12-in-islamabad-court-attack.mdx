A suicide bomb exploded outside Islamabad's district courts, killing 12 people and wounding 27. The Pakistan Taliban, a militant group, claimed responsibility and said they target judges and lawyers who support what they call un‑Islamic laws. The attack highlights Pakistan’s rising terror problem and raises doubts about peace talks with the Afghan Taliban. This incident has shocked the nation and forced the government to declare a state of war.
