After U.S. and Israel bombed Iranian officials and killed the country's supreme leader, President Trump said he was ready to talk with Iran's remaining leaders. The fighting has spread to Lebanon and hit cities, killing civilians and even a school. Iran says it won’t negotiate and has sent missiles at U.S. bases. The war has raised oil prices and hurt global shipping. Trump says he could still talk but warns of more U.S. casualties.
