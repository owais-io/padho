Three U.S. soldiers died during Operation Epic Fury, the first confirmed American casualties after the U.S. began striking Iran on Saturday. Five others were seriously wounded. The strikes, carried out with Israel, hit Iranian targets, including a house linked to former president Ahmadinejad, and follow threats from U.S. President Trump to use unprecedented force if Iran retaliates. A separate Iranian missile attack on a girls’ school killed nearly 150 people, sparking protests in the U.S. against the war.
