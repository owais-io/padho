Because of the war in Iran, many people have left the Middle East and taken Dubai with them. They’re abandoning their cats, dogs and other pets, and animal charities are struggling to keep up. Shelters are full, rescuers are overwhelmed, and the UK government has helped some families bring their pets home. The UAE has started AI‑powered feeding stations to help the stray animals.
