In a tense day‑two clash at Lord’s, England’s bowlers Archer and Bumrah kept India on their toes. Archer ripped out Yashasvi Jaiswal early, finishing with 1‑22, while Bumrah claimed his first five‑wicket haul at the venue, helping England bowl India out for 145. England’s top order, especially Root’s 104 and Stokes’ sharp catch, set a 387‑run target that left India fighting hard but still behind.
