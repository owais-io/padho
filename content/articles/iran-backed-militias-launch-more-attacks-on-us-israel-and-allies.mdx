Militias tied to Iran are stepping up strikes on Israel, the United States, and their allies, especially in Iraq. They have fired drones, missiles and rockets at US bases in Jordan, Iraq and Kuwait, and hit infrastructure used by Iranian‑Kurdish opposition groups. Israel and the US have responded with airstrikes and ground missions to hit the militias’ bases. Iraq says it wants to stay out of the fighting, but attacks keep rising as the proxy war in the region expands.
