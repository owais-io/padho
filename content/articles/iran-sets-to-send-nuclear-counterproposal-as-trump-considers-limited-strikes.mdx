After talks in Geneva, Iran’s foreign minister says a draft counterproposal on its nuclear program will be ready in a few days. Meanwhile, President Trump is weighing limited strikes on Tehran, while the US has moved warships and carriers to the Middle East. The military buildup is meant to pressure Iran into a deal, but Iran insists there is no military solution to its nuclear ambitions.
