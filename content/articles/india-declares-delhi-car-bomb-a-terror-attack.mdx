On Monday, a car bomb exploded near Delhi’s Red Fort, killing 12 people and injuring more than 30. The Indian government said the blast was a terrorist act by “anti‑national forces” and is now a formal terror case. Police say they found a large stash of explosives and linked the incident to a Jaish‑e‑Mohammad cell in Kashmir, with five suspects arrested. The attack follows a previous April shooting and has raised tensions with Pakistan.
