Britain announced it will tighten sanctions against Iran after protests that killed and arrested many people. The new rules will hit Iran’s money, energy, transport and software sectors. The UK’s foreign secretary met the Iranian ambassador to condemn the violence. The move follows a US tariff and EU talks about more sanctions. Britain wants to pressure the Iranian regime and support people fighting for freedom.
