A 23‑year‑old Iranian student, Rubina Aminian, was shot in the head during protests in Tehran. The shooting happened just after she left school. Her family had to travel from their home in western Iran to find her body among many others. Police blocked a proper burial, so her relatives buried her on a road between cities. Over 500 people have died in the protests, and more than 10,000 have been arrested.
