People across the UK gave their take on Chancellor Rachel Reeves’s spring budget talk. Nobody announced big tax cuts or new spending, so many were left anxious. Students with huge loans are still stuck paying interest that keeps growing. Workers worry that a Middle East conflict could push up fuel, food and mortgage costs even more. Some called for cheaper borrowing, lower interest rates and a look at supermarket prices.
