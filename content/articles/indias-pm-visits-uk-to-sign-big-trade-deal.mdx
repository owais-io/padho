India’s Prime Minister Narendra Modi is in London to sign a major free trade agreement with the UK. The deal is the UK’s biggest post‑Brexit trade pact and India’s first big trade deal outside Asia. It will remove most tariffs on Indian goods and gradually cut duties on UK products, boosting jobs and investment. The agreement still needs parliament approval and skips some sensitive areas like agriculture and a carbon tax.
