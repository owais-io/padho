WHO reports that at least 13 hospitals and other health sites in Iran, plus one in Lebanon, have been struck during recent U.S. and Israeli attacks. Four medics were killed and 25 injured. The fighting has caused over 1,200 deaths in Iran, many injuries, and forced 100,000 people to leave Tehran. It also shut down WHO’s emergency logistics hub in Dubai, delaying life‑saving supplies for the region.
