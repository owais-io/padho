People living at Royal Mint Court say they will sue if the council approves China’s plan to build a huge embassy there. Residents fear they could lose their homes and the new building would house 230 Chinese staff and guests. They have raised £37,000 toward a lawyer and will file a judicial review in the High Court if the decision goes against them. The case highlights worries about security and unfair planning rules.
