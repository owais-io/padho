After the Iranian government shut down the internet, a photographer in Tehran was able to publish pictures of the violence that followed. Witnesses describe crowds chanting, tear gas, and bullets fired at them. People were injured, killed, and many are left with pellets in their bodies. The footage shows police and militia officers shooting at both adults and children, turning the protests into a deadly confrontation.
