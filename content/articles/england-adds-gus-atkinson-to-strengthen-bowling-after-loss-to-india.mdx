England have called up young pacer Gus Atkinson for the third Test against India because they need fresh bowlers after a heavy defeat at Edgbaston. Ben Stokes said the team had to recover quickly, with only a few days before the next match at Lord’s. Atkinson hasn't played since May due to a hamstring injury, but he’s expected to be fit. England’s bowling group will also include Sam Cook and Jofra Archer.
