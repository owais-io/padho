After a US aircraft carrier and destroyers moved into the Mediterranean, Iran is preparing for a possible missile strike by the US and Israel. The move comes amid violent protests in Iran and a record drop in its stock market. Iran says it will monitor all moves and warns that any attack would damage regional stability. Diplomats say no deal is in sight.
