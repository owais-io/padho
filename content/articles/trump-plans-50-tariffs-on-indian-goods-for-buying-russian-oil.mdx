Trump has slapped a 25% extra duty on Indian imports, bringing the total to 50%, because India is buying Russian oil. India has 21 days to say no before the new tariff kicks in. The U.S. says India fuels the war in Ukraine, while India calls the move unfair and vows to protect its interests. The move comes as Trump also targets Brazil, Canada and Mexico with high tariffs.
