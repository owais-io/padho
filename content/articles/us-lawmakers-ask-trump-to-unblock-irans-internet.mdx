US lawmakers have written to President Trump, asking him to let the US help Iranian people use the internet again. The request comes after huge protests in Iran that have killed thousands. The lawmakers want Trump to work with the Open Technology Fund to unblock censorship, using tools like VPNs. They say the Freedom Act in Congress could do this but it hasn’t passed yet, so they need the president’s help now.
