Trump announced a $100,000 fee for H‑1B work visas that let Indian tech workers move to the US. India says the fee would hurt families and calls it a threat to its talent. The move shocked tech firms, sent shares of Infosys and Tata back down, and caused confusion among workers who wondered if they could still travel or stay in the US. Indian officials urge the US to fix the problem quickly.
