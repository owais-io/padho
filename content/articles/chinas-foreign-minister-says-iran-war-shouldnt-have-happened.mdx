Wang Yi, China’s top diplomat, said the war in the Middle East, especially the US‑Israel attacks on Iran, should never have happened. He urged the United States to respect China and keep trade talks going, ahead of Donald Trump’s expected visit. China stayed silent on the US‑Iran strikes, focusing instead on improving China‑US ties. He also warned about Taiwan and criticised Japan’s military stance.
