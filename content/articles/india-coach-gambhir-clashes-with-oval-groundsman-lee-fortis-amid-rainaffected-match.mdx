During England’s fifth Test at the Oval, India’s coach Gautam Gambhir got into a spat with groundsman Lee Fortis. After a practice session, Gambhir insulted Fortis, calling him “nothing”, which sparked a social‑media frenzy. Fortis defended himself, and the incident highlighted tensions between high‑profile players and ground staff. It showed how a single comment can turn into a global meme and expose power dynamics in cricket.
