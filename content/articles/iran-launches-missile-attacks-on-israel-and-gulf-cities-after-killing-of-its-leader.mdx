Iran has launched a new wave of missile and drone attacks on Israel and Gulf cities after its long‑time leader, Supreme Leader Ali Khamenei, was killed. Israel and the U.S. responded with air strikes that have hit Tehran and other parts of Iran, killing high‑ranking officials and injuring civilians. The attacks have sparked protests across Iran, raised fears of a wider war, and prompted a U.N. meeting on nuclear security.
