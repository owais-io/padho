England and India played a grueling five‑day Test in scorching heat, with rain‑like sweat and constant interruptions. Injuries and a tense atmosphere saw umpire Sharfuddoula Saikat remove a player early. England's bowler Zak Crawley sparked a row with India’s Shubman Gill after a shaky delivery and a finger injury. India’s coach Gautam Gambhir’s hard‑ball approach is driving the team’s fierce play, while England struggles to regain rhythm after a break.
