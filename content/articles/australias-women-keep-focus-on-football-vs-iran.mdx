Australia’s women’s coach Joe Montemurro says the team wants to keep the focus on football for their Asian Cup match against Iran, even as tensions rise in the Middle East. After a 1‑0 win over the Philippines, the Matildas will face Iran on Thursday. Montemurro urges reporters to avoid politics, while the AFC also shut down a question for Iran’s coach. Injuries left the squad thin, but Sam Kerr scored the match’s only goal.
