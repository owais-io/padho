Experts warn that U.S. airstrikes would fuel the Iranian government's narrative that the unrest is a Western plot and could cause more civilian harm. No clear plan is ready, and allies like Qatar urge restraint. Iran's leaders say the regime can't solve people's problems, while the U.S. faces doubts about its ability to carry out a successful strike.
