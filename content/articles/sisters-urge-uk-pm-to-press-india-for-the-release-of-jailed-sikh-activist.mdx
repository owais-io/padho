The sisters of human‑rights activist Alaa Abd el‑Fattah are asking UK Prime Minister Keir Starmer to tell Indian Prime Minister Narendra Modi to free Jagtar Singh Johal, a British Sikh held in India for almost eight years. Johal has never been convicted and was cleared in one case, but he still faces serious charges. The letter, joined by other former detainees, says his continued imprisonment hurts UK‑India relations and needs urgent action.
