German 20‑year‑old Vincent Keymer played in Chennai, India, and won 7 of 9 games without a loss. He beat several top Indian grandmasters, including Arjun Erigaisi, and rose into the world’s top 10 for the first time, passing a 2750 rating. Keymer had already knocked out world No 2 Magnus Carlsen in a previous tournament. His victory shows a young German challenger ready to fight for the world title.
