A former Iranian diplomat in Canberra, Mohammad Pournajaf, applied for asylum in 2023 and has now been granted protection in Australia. He had been the charge d'affaires at Iran’s embassy and served in other posts, but decided to stay after leaving the country in 2018. The move comes amid other diplomatic tensions, including the expulsion of Iran’s ambassador after accusations of anti‑Semitic attacks. Meanwhile, a group of Iranian women footballers were also given humanitarian visas to stay in Australia.
