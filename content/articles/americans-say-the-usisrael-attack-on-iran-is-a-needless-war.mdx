After the U.S. and Israel bombed Iranian targets on Feb. 28, many Americans told the Guardian they disapproved. A retired cardiologist from Tehran said the war is pointless and could drag U.S. troops into a losing fight. A Brooklyn shop owner felt the strike hurt her Muslim friends during Ramadan, while a North Carolina counselor blamed Trump for starting a selfish war. Comments focus on civilian deaths, lack of a clear plan, and the risk of another Middle‑East conflict.
