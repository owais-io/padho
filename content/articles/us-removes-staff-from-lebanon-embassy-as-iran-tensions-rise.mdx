The U.S. has sent 30‑50 non‑essential employees out of its Beirut embassy because it fears an Iranian attack if tensions turn into war. While the embassy still runs on a core team, officials say the move keeps people safe. Washington has also moved ships and planes close to Iran, preparing for possible strikes, and is holding talks with Iran about its nuclear program.
