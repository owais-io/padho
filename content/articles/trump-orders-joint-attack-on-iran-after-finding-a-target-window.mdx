Trump ordered a joint strike with Israel against Iran after they found a chance to hit the country's top leaders, including Supreme Leader Khamenei. The move was meant to weaken Iran’s power and stop its missile and nuclear build‑up, which U.S. officials say is a big threat. Talks in Geneva with Iranian officials failed, and Oman’s diplomat said the attacks hurt peace talks. Trump’s decision followed his withdrawal from the 2015 nuclear deal.
