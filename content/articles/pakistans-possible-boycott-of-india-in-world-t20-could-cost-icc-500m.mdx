The ICC faces a major problem as Pakistan may refuse to play India on February 15 in the T20 World Cup. Pakistan’s boycott, sparked by the ICC’s earlier ban of Bangladesh, threatens a $500 million media‑rights deal and could lead to fines, point deductions, and a loss of revenue for the ICC and smaller cricket nations. Negotiations are still ongoing, but the situation is urgent.
