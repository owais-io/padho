During the opening day of the fourth Test at Old Trafford, India's wicket‑keeper Rishabh Pant twisted his foot while attempting a reverse sweep and had to leave the field on a golf buggy. England’s captain Ben Stokes used the occasion to bowl well, taking early wickets including Shubman Gill and Sai Sudharsan, and pushing India to 212/3. The match ended with England ahead, and Pant’s injury left India uncertain about his role in the rest of the game.
