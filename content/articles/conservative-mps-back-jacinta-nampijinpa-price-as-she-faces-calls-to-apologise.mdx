Jacinta Nampijinpa Price, a Northern Territory senator, said Indian migrants were treated better in the migration system, sparking anger from the Indian community. Some conservatives, like Sarah Henderson and Moira Deeming, have publicly defended her, saying she didn’t hurt anyone and should keep the dialogue open. Others, including the Liberal leader Sussan Ley and Prime Minister Anthony Albanese, want her to apologise. The dispute has exposed deep rifts inside the Coalition.
