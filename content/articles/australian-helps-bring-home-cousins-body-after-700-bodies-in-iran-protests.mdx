Payam, who left Iran for Australia, learned his cousin Eli was shot dead during protests in Karaj. He says family had to choose between paying $9,000 to get the body or sign a form saying the death was by civilians. He described the horror of seeing 700 bodies and the fear his relatives now feel. The story shows how the Iranian crackdown is hurting families abroad.
