A UK company that rents private jets, Air Charter Service, says it’s getting 200‑300% more requests than usual after the Iran war started. Thousands of people, including tourists and British nationals, are stuck in the Middle East and need a quick exit. The company helps governments and rich clients alike, offering whole planes or selling seats. Flights from places like Oman are being organised, but delays and limited planes keep people waiting.
