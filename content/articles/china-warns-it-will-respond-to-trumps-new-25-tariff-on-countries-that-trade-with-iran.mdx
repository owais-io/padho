Trump said he will add a 25% tax on any nation that trades with Iran, with no exemptions. China, which buys most of Iran’s oil, says it will take “all necessary measures” to protect its interests. The tariff could hurt trade between the two countries and spark a wider trade war. Trump also hinted at other sanctions and possible military options while the U.S. Supreme Court may challenge his tariff powers.
