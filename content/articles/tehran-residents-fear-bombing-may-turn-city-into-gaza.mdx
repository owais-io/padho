After a night of intense bombing in Tehran, locals said it was the worst in six days of war. With internet shut down, people shared fear, exhaustion, and stories of explosions that shook windows and rattled streets. A teacher, a former protester and a reporter all described how the strikes left them terrified, wondering if the city could become like Gaza. Many residents are leaving the capital, hoping the attacks stop.
