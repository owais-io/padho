Israeli forces hit Iranian sites and the Iran‑backed group Hezbollah, killing dozens and displacing thousands in Lebanon. Iran reports hundreds of deaths across the country, and the U.S. says it has hit over 1,250 targets. Evacuations are being organized for U.S. and other citizens in the region.
