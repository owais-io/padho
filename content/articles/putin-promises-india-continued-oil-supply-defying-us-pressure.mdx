Putin met India’s Prime Minister Narendra Modi in Delhi and promised to keep sending oil to India, even after the U.S. slapped a 25% tax on Russian oil bought by India. The Russian leader said this shows Russia won’t back down from U.S. pressure. India said the partnership is key for its energy security. The two countries also signed deals to boost trade and defence ties.
