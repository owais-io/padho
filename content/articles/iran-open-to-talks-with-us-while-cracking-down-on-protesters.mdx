Iran’s foreign minister says his country is ready to negotiate with the U.S. about its nuclear program, but only if the talks are respectful. President Trump claims a meeting is being set up, though he warns that the U.S. might need to act because Iran is violently suppressing protests. The U.S. has threatened a military strike and wants sanctions lifted, while Iran says it will only negotiate if the U.S. stops hostile actions.
