Trump told Fox News that Iran is in talks with the U.S. and that a deal might keep the U.S. from using military force. A U.S. naval group led by the carrier Abraham Lincoln is already off Iran’s coast. Iranian leaders warn any attack could spark a regional war and say they’re ready to negotiate over nukes but not missiles. Tensions rise as protests rage in Iran and U.S. officials weigh options.
