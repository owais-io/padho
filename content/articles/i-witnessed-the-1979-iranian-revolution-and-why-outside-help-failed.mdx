Back in 1979, I was a young reporter in Paris and got a brief meeting with Ayatollah Khomeini, the future leader of Iran. I watched the Shah’s government fall and the new Islamic Republic form. Khomeini set up secret police and the Revolutionary Guard, giving himself real power. The U.S. tried to help the Shah, but the revolution won and stayed strong. Today the same pattern of foreign interference repeats.
