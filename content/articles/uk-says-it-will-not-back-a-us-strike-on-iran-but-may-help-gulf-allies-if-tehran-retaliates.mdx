The UK says it will not support a US attack on Iran. Instead, Britain has moved Typhoon jets to Qatar to help Gulf allies if Iran retaliates. The move follows a US buildup near the region. UK officials say the deployment follows a Qatari request and is in line with international law. They warn that Britain will defend allies only if they need help, while staying focused on stopping Iran’s nuclear programme.
