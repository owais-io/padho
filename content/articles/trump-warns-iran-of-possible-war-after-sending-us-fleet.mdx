President Donald Trump has warned that a war with Iran could happen soon and has sent a large US naval fleet, including the aircraft carrier USS Abraham Lincoln, toward the region. He demands Iran drop its nuclear program and negotiate a deal without pre‑conditions, threatening a “big strike” if it does not. Iran says it will defend itself if pushed, and other Gulf and Turkish diplomats are trying to calm tensions.
