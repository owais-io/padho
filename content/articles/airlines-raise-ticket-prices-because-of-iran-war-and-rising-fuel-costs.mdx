The war between the US and Israel over Iran has pushed oil prices up, and airlines are passing the extra fuel cost on to passengers. Cathay Pacific, AirAsia, Thai Airways and others are adding fuel surcharges and raising fares, especially on long‑haul routes that used to go through the Middle East. Some airlines have cancelled flights and warned that prices could climb 10‑30%. Travelers are advised to book early to avoid steep hikes.
