England struggled in hot conditions at Lord's as India's bowlers, especially Bumrah, made them play very slowly. The match was slow, with few runs scored after lunch. The England captain, Joe Stokes, had to change his usual attacking style. India’s bowlers showed they can win quickly if they find the right line. Fans saw a lot of waiting time as ball changes took a long time.
