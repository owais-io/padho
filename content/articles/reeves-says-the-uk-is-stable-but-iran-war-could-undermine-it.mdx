Rachel Reeves told MPs the UK’s economy is on track, citing lower inflation and stronger public finances. But the news came right after a warning that British citizens might need to be evacuated from fighting in Iran, and oil prices are climbing. Higher energy costs could push inflation back up, and the Office for Budget Responsibility says the current forecasts may become outdated if the war lasts.
