Dozens of soldiers and civilians died when fighting broke out again on the Afghanistan‑Pakistan border. Pakistan said Taliban forces fired on border posts, then hit back with mortar and drone attacks that also hit Taliban bases in Kandahar and Kabul. The fighting killed at least 20 Taliban fighters and several civilians. Residents fled villages and schools were hit. After the bloodshed both sides agreed to a 48‑hour ceasefire, hoping to talk it out.
