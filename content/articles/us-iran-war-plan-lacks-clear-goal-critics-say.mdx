US and Israeli jets started attacks on Iran, but the plan was chaotic. Trump’s brief plan for regime change ran into a lack of clear goals, leading to mis‑targeted strikes, civilian deaths, and the killing of key Iranian leaders. The war cost over $11 bn in the first six days, closed the Strait of Hormuz, drove up oil prices, and left no clear path for the US to win or recover. Critics warn this may hurt U.S. power for years.
