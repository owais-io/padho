Last week, protests in Iran were met with violence. President Trump warned that the U.S. could respond with force if Tehran escalates. White House press secretary Karoline Leavitt said airstrikes are an option, but diplomacy is still the priority. Trump also announced a 25% tariff on all trade with Iran. The move follows Iranian protests and the U.S. has already struck nuclear sites last year.
