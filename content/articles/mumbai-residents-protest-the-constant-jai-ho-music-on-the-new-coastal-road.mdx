Mumbai’s new Coastal Road has a ‘musical’ stretch that plays the song ‘Jai Ho’ when cars drive slowly. Residents in the upscale Breach Candy area say the constant music disturbs their homes and causes stress. Over 650 families have complained, asking authorities to stop it. Officials say the grooves help keep drivers safe and slow, but critics argue the noise is a distraction and only benefits wealthy drivers on the expensive motorway.
