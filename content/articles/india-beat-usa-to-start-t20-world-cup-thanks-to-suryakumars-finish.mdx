In the tournament’s first game, India scored 161 for 9 while the U.S. managed 132. Suryakumar Yadav’s late surge of 84 off 49 gave India the lead, despite a shaky start and several missed chances. The U.S. lost early wickets and couldn’t recover, with key Indian bowlers like Mohammed Siraj and Arshdeep Singh taking wickets early. India’s win was a solid start to their campaign.
