Since the Taliban took power in Afghanistan, the Pakistan Taliban (TTP) have stepped up attacks on police, soldiers, and civilians in the border region. Police officers like Tariq Ahmed live in constant fear after their colleagues are killed or bombed. The group has gained new weapons and drones left by the US, making them harder to fight. The Pakistani government blames the Afghan Taliban for giving the TTP safe havens, while locals say the military’s strikes hurt civilians too.
