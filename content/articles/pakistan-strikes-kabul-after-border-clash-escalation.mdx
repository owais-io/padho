Pakistan fired airstrikes into Kabul, Paktia and Kandahar after Afghan forces attacked Pakistani border troops. Pakistani officials say the raids killed 133 Taliban leaders and wounded 200, while Afghanistan reports 55 Pakistani soldiers dead. Both sides have called for war and threatened more attacks. The UN urges protection of civilians and diplomacy. Refugees near the border are being moved to safer areas amid the renewed fighting.
