Doctors in Tehran say more than 400 protesters were shot in the eyes and heads as authorities cracked down on nightly protests. Hospitals are full, staff treat wounded in bad conditions, and many patients lose vision or have eyes removed. Doctors claim the shots targeted eyes. The government shut down the internet, making it hard to get help, and has killed over 2,000 people so far. Protesters keep marching despite the violence.
