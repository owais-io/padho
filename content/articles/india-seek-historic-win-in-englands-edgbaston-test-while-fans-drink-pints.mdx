England and India faced off at Edgbaston after a brief heatwave. Fans arrived dressed in odd outfits, and the crowd paused to honour the late cricketer Wayne Larkins. The match ended in a historic draw for India, their only undefeated Test at the ground. A glare from a window delayed play when opener Yashasvi Jaiswal slipped. Despite long queues, fans enjoyed plenty of food stalls and pints.
