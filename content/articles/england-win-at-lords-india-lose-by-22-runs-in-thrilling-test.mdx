England beat India in a tense third Test at Lord’s, winning by just 22 runs and taking a 2‑1 lead in the series. Ben Stokes was the star, bowling key wickets and making game‑changing runs, while Jofra Archer returned to take two wickets after a long injury break. India’s Ravindra Jadeja almost saved the match with a long‑lasting 61, but a late ball that fell on the stumps sealed the win for England.
