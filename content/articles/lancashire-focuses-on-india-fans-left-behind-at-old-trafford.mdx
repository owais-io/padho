Lancashire Cricket is chasing Indian fans and business by hosting a welcome event for the new Indian consulate, opening a channel on JioTV, and planning tours and an IPL partnership. Meanwhile, local fans were frustrated by long queues and low ticket sales at the Old Trafford Test. The county also faces internal turmoil with coaching changes and a weak County Championship run, while still celebrating recent women’s T20 success.
