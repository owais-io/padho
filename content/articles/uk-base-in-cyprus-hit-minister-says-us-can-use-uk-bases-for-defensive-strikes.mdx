An unmanned drone hit the runway at RAF Akrotiri in Cyprus, but no one was hurt. UK ministers said the country is not at war and is only allowing the US to use UK bases for defensive strikes against Iranian missile launches that threaten Gulf allies, many of whom are British. The UK is not supporting any offensive attacks and is helping Britons in the Gulf get home safely.
