Donald Trump said Iran’s top officials have agreed to talk after the U.S. and Israel launched strikes that killed several Iranian leaders, including the supreme leader. He said the talks should have happened earlier and praised the attacks, claiming they had removed 48 key figures. Trump said the military campaign is running ahead of schedule and could finish in about four weeks. He added that the outcome would be “great” for the world.
