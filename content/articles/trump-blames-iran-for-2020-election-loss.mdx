He linked to a Trump‑friendly article saying Iranian spies tried to hurt his campaign. Trump has made similar posts about Venezuela, spreading unproven claims. While intelligence agencies say Iran hacked campaign data, there's no proof it caused the loss. The White House didn’t respond.
