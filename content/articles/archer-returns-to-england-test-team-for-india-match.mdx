England’s fast bowler Jofra Archer will play his first Test since 2021, the only change to the squad for the third match against India at Lord’s. Archer has battled elbow and back injuries but is ready after a recent county game. Fast bowler Mark Wood, who has a knee injury, is hoping to return for the final Test. Both sides have complained about the ball’s quality, with Indian players saying the balls soften too quickly.
