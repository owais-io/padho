Ali Karimi and other former Iranian football players, a coach, a referee and journalists have written an open letter to FIFA president Gianni Infantino, asking him to condemn the killings, arrests and threats against athletes during protests in Iran. They say more than 18,000 people have died, including many football fans and players, and that the government is repressing peaceful protest. The letter urges FIFA to publicly denounce the violence and protect Iranian athletes.
