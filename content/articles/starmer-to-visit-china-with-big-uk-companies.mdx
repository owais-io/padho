UK Prime Minister Keir Starmer will head a delegation of major British firms to China next week, following the UK government’s approval of a new Chinese embassy in London. The visit is part of a revived UK‑China CEO council that includes companies such as BP, HSBC, and Rolls‑Royce. The trip comes amid protests over the embassy’s planning and a broader debate about ties with China, after a spy‑case controversy. China says the embassy is built following legal rules.
