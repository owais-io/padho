In the second Twenty‑20 match, England chased a revised target of 168 after rain cut the game down. Harry Brook scored a brisk 36 off 12 balls to spark the chase, while Tom Banton added an unbeaten 54. Sam Curran sealed the win with a late finish, giving England a 2‑0 series victory and a clean slate before the World Cup.
