Trump said the Iranian team should not play in the 2026 World Cup in the U.S. for their own safety, even though FIFA said they were welcome. The comment came after the U.S. started bombing Iran. Iran’s sports minister said the team would not enter the tournament. The team’s matches were set for Los Angeles and Seattle, but the situation is still uncertain.
