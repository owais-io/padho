President Trump joined Israel in launching an air‑and‑naval attack on Iran without asking Congress for approval. Republican leaders in both the House and Senate blocked any war‑powers resolution that would require congressional permission, saying the president has the authority to act. The conflict has killed U.S. and Iranian civilians, cost billions in the first days, and is unpopular with voters. Democrats criticize the move and plan to use the war to rally support in the midterms.
