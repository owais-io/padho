After news that President Trump ordered airstrikes on Tehran that were said to kill Iran’s supreme leader, people across the United States marched to protest. The demonstrations, held outside the White House, Times Square and many other cities, were organized by left‑wing groups and the ACLU. Protesters demanded Congress stop the attacks, saying the military action is illegal and would bring more war to the region.
