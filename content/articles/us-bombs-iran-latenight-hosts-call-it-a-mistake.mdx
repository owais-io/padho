Trump ordered a US attack on Iran after killing its leader, and late‑night hosts mocked the decision. They said the war had no clear purpose, no end in sight, and was carried out without Congress approval. The hosts poked fun at Trump’s vague justifications, the lack of an exit plan, and the confusing way the president announced the strike. The satire highlights how chaotic and unplanned the attack feels.
