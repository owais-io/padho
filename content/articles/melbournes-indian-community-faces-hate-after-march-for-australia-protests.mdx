After the March for Australia rallies, many Indian‑Australian residents in Melbourne say they feel targeted by hate. Leaders met to warn about racist flyers that singled out South Asians and about physical attacks on students. Police said neo‑Nazi groups were involved. Community members reported online threats, vandalised temples, and even a train‑car assault. The state government is meeting with anti‑hate groups to address the surge in racism.
