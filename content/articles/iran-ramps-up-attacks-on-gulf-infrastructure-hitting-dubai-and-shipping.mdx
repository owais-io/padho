Iran has stepped up bombings of civilian infrastructure and transport in the Gulf, striking Dubai's airport and commercial ships. US and Israeli warplanes hit back, and Iran warned of a long war of attrition that could flood the world with a crisis. Oil prices jumped over $100 a barrel before dropping again. Most attacks hit the UAE, leaving Dubai's tourism and finance sectors under threat.
