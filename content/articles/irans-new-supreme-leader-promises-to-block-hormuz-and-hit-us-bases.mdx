After the death of his father, Iran’s new Supreme Leader Mojtaba Khamenei addressed the nation on state TV. He vowed to keep the Strait of Hormuz closed and to keep striking U.S. military bases in the region, demanding compensation for attacks on Iran. He praised hard‑liners, called for national unity, and thanked the Houthis and Hezbollah for their support. Khamenei also mentioned the recent attacks that killed his family members and warned Israel that it could face retaliation.
