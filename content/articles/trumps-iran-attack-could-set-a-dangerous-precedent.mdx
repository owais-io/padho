The U.S. and Israel shot down Iran’s top leader, a move the Guardian says is an illegal war that could set a new rule that any country can strike another to stop a future threat. The attack wasn’t backed by Congress or the UN, and it could spark more fighting in the Middle East. Iran has threatened to close a key oil route, and prices could soar. The piece warns that normalising such attacks could harm global peace and rules.
