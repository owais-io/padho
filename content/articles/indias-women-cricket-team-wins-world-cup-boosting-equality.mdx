India’s women’s cricket team just won the World Cup, a historic win that shows sports can help gender equality. Players like Shafali Verma grew up facing gender bans, but they now stand on a global stage. The victory drew huge crowds, even in villages that used to ignore women’s games. Fans say it will change attitudes, encourage more girls to play, and give India a stronger push for equal treatment.
